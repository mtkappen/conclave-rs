# Epic Transformation

**Level:** 10  
**Traditions:** Arcane, Primal, Transmutation  
**Cast:** [two-actions] somatic, verbal  
**Duration:** 1 minute  

You transform into a being of epic power, gaining abilities far beyond normal mortal limits. You gain the following benefits:

- AC = 28 + your level. Ignore your armor's check penalty and Speed reduction.
- 40 temporary Hit Points.

- All your Speeds increase by 30 feet.
- You gain darkvision and low-light vision.

- Your unarmed attacks deal an additional 4d10 damage of your choice (acid, cold, electricity, fire, force, or sonic).
- You gain resistance 15 to all damage types except force and ghost touch.

- You can cast any spell you know as if it were heightened to one level higher than its actual level, up to 9th level.

Additionally, once per round when you Cast a Spell, you can choose to have it affect twice the normal number of targets or double its area of effect.
**Heightened (+1)** The temporary Hit Points increase by 10 and the resistance increases by 5.
