# Anathematic Reprisal

## Spell 4 - Enchantment Mental
**Traditions:** divine  
**Cast:** [reaction] somatic, verbal; **Trigger:** A creature performs an act anathema to your deity.  
**Range:** 30 feet  
**Targets:** the triggering creature  
**Saving Throw:** Will  

You punish a creature that transgresses against your deity, drawing upon the anguish you feel upon seeing one of your deity's anathema committed. You deal 4d6 mental damage to the target, but a basic Will save can reduce this damage. If it fails, it is also stupefied 1 for 1 round. The creature is then temporarily immune for 1 minute.
**Heightened (+1):** The damage increases by 1d6.
