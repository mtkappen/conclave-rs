# Summon Construct

**Level:** 1  
**Traditions:** Arcane  
**Cast:** [three-actions] material, somatic, verbal  
**Range:** 30 feet  
**Duration:** sustained up to 1 minute  

You conjure a construct to fight for you. This works like summon animal, except you summon a common creature that has the construct trait and whose level is -1.

Heightened As summon animal.
