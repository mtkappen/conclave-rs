# Lore (Intelligence)

Lore represents specialized knowledge in a particular field. Each Lore skill has a specific subcategory (such as Alcohol Lore, Law Lore, or Local Lore). You can have multiple Lore skills, each representing expertise in a different area.

## Untrained Actions

### Recall Knowledge
Recall Knowledge about the specific subject of your Lore skill's subcategory. For example:
- **Business Lore:** Commerce, trade routes, market trends
- **Law Lore:** Legal systems, court procedures, statutes
- **Sailing Lore:** Ships, navigation, maritime customs
- **Tea Lore:** Tea varieties, brewing methods, tea culture

The GM determines what specific topics fall under each Lore subcategory.

## Trained Actions

### Earn Income - DOWNTIME
You use your specialized knowledge to earn money during downtime. This works like the general Earn Income activity, but specifically through applying your Lore expertise. Common uses include:
- Consulting on matters related to your specialty
- Teaching or lecturing about your area of expertise
- Providing expert analysis or appraisal

The GM determines the task level based on how lucrative and specialized the work is.

- **Critical Success:** You deliver exceptional work that earns premium pay; earn income as if the task were 1 level higher.
- **Success:** You deliver quality work that earns standard pay for the task level.
- **Failure:** Your work is mediocre and earns reduced pay or might not find clients at all.
- **Critical Failure:** Your work contains significant errors that damage your reputation; you earn nothing and might face a –2 penalty to future Earn Income attempts using this Lore skill.

**Sample Lore Earn Income Tasks:**
| Task | Proficiency |
|------|-------------|
| Basic consulting or research | Trained |
| Expert analysis or teaching | Expert |
| High-level consulting or specialized work | Master |
| World-renowned expertise or unique knowledge | Legendary |

## Choosing Lore Skills

When you gain training in a Lore skill, choose a specific subcategory. Common options include:
- **Local Lore:** History and personalities of a specific region
- **Business/Commerce Lore:** Trade, markets, and economic matters
- **Law Lore:** Legal systems and procedures
- **Sailing/Nautical Lore:** Ships, navigation, maritime customs
- **Underworld Lore:** Criminal organizations, illegal activities
- **Nobility Lore:** Aristocratic families, court politics
- **Craft-specific Lore:** Blacksmithing, weaving, alchemy, etc.

You can gain additional Lore skills through feats (such as the Additional Lore feat) or by gaining training from your background or class. Each Lore skill is treated as a separate skill for all purposes.
