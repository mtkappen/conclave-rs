# Crafting Skill Feats

## Level 1 Feats

### Alchemical Crafting FEAT 1
**Prerequisites:** trained in Crafting  
Craft alchemical items. When you select this feat, add formulas for four common 1st-level alchemical items to your formula book.

### Quick Repair FEAT 1
**Prerequisites:** trained in Crafting  
Repair items quickly; reduce time required for Repair by half.

### Snare Crafting FEAT 1
**Prerequisites:** trained in Crafting  
Craft snares; gain access to basic snare formulas.

### Specialty Crafting FEAT 1
**Prerequisites:** trained in Crafting  
Gain bonuses to Craft certain items; choose a category (weapons, armor, etc.) and gain +2 circumstance bonus.

## Level 2 Feats

### Magical Crafting FEAT 2
**Prerequisites:** expert in Crafting  
Craft magic items; you can now create magical items using the Craft activity.

## Level 7 Feats

### Impeccable Crafting FEAT 7
**Prerequisites:** master in Crafting, Specialty Crafting  
Craft items more efficiently; reduce material costs by 10%.

### Inventor FEAT 7
**Prerequisites:** master in Crafting  
Use Crafting to create item formulas; you can invent new alchemical or mundane items.

## Level 15 Feats

### Craft Anything FEAT 15
**Prerequisites:** legendary in Crafting  
Ignore most requirements for crafting items; you can craft items even without meeting level or spell prerequisites (except cost-based ones).
