# Nature (Wisdom)

Nature measures your knowledge of the natural world, including flora, fauna, weather, geography, and planar environments.

## Untrained Actions

### Recall Knowledge
Recall Knowledge about:
- The environment, flora, geography, weather
- Creatures of natural origin
- Natural planes (Elemental Planes, Maelstrom, etc.)

## Trained Actions

### Command an Animal [one-action]
**Requirements:** You can see and communicate with the animal, and it must be able to understand your commands.

You attempt to direct an animal's behavior. Make a Nature check against the animal's Will DC. This works best on animals that aren't hostile toward you.

- **Critical Success:** The animal follows your command precisely and might even take additional helpful actions.
- **Success:** The animal follows your command as best it can.
- **Failure:** The animal ignores your command or misunderstands it.
- **Critical Failure:** The animal becomes agitated or hostile toward you.

**Sample Commands:**
| Command | Typical DC |
|---------|------------|
| Simple (sit, stay, come) | 10-15 |
| Moderate (attack, guard, fetch) | 15-20 |
| Complex (specific tactics, rescue) | 20-25 |

### Identify Magic - EXPLORATION SECRET
Using Nature, you can attempt to identify primal magic, including druidic spells, primal magical items, and creatures with primal abilities. This works like the general Identify Magic action but specifically applies to primal traditions.

- **Critical Success:** You learn all attributes of the primal magic, including its name, effects, and any means of activating it.
- **Success:** For an item or location, you get a sense of what it does and learn any means of activating it. For an ongoing effect, you learn the effect's name and what it does.
- **Failure:** You fail to identify the magic and can't try again for 1 day.
- **Critical Failure:** You misidentify the magic as something else.

### Learn a Spell - EXPLORATION
If you're a primal spellcaster (such as a druid or ranger), you can use Nature to learn a new spell from the primal tradition. This works like the general Learn a Spell action but uses Nature instead of Arcana, Religion, or Occultism.

To learn the spell:
- Spend 1 hour per level of the spell
- Have materials with the Price indicated in Table 4–3 (Learning a Spell)
- Attempt a Nature check against the DC determined by the GM
