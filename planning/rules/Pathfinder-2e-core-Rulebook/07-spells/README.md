## Spell Traditions

Spells belong to one or more magical traditions:
- **Arcane**: Logic-based magic, broadest spell list (wizards, sorcerers)
- **Divine**: Faith-based magic from deities (clerics, champions)
- **Occult**: Mystery and esoteric knowledge (bards, occult sorcerers)
- **Primal**: Nature-based magic (druids, primal sorcerers)

## Spell Schools

All spells belong to one of eight schools:
- **Abjuration**: Protection and warding
- **Conjuration**: Summoning and teleportation
- **Divination**: Knowledge and foresight
- **Enchantment**: Mind-affecting magic
- **Evocation**: Energy manipulation
- **Illusion**: Deception and trickery
- **Necromancy**: Life, death, and healing
- **Transmutation**: Transformation and alteration

## Reading Spell Entries

Each spell entry includes:
- **Name and Level**: Spell name with level indicator
- **Traits**: School, tradition, and other traits
- **Cast Time**: Actions required to cast
- **Range/Area**: How far or wide the spell affects
- **Targets**: Who or what the spell affects
- **Duration**: How long the spell lasts
- **Saving Throw**: What save targets can attempt
- **Description**: Full spell effects
- **Heightened**: Effects when cast at higher levels
