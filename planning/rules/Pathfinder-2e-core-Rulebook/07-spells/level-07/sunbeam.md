# Sunbeam

**Level:** 7  
**Traditions:** Divine, Primal  
**Cast:** [two-actions] somatic, verbal  
**Range:** 120 feet; Area: 5-foot line, 60 feet long  
**Saving Throw:** Reflex  

A brilliant beam of sunlight shoots from your hand. Each creature in the line must attempt a Reflex save. The spell deals 8d6 fire damage and 8d6 positive damage to undead creatures.

Additionally, if the beam passes through an area of magical darkness or targets a creature affected by magical darkness, sunbeam attempts to counteract the darkness effect.
**Heightened (+1)** The fire damage increases by 1d6, and the positive damage against undead increases by 1d6.
