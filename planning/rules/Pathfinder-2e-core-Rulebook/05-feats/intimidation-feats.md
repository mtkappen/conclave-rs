# Intimidation Skill Feats

## Level 1 Feats
- **Group Coercion FEAT 1** - Coerce multiple targets simultaneously
- **Intimidating Glare FEAT 1** - Demoralize a creature without speaking
- **Quick Coercion FEAT 1** - Coerce a creature quickly

## Level 2 Feats
- **Intimidating Prowess FEAT 2** (Str 16, Expert) - Gain a bonus to physically Demoralize a target
- **Lasting Coercion FEAT 2** (Expert) - Coerce a target into helping you longer

## Level 7 Feats
- **Battle Cry FEAT 7** (Master) - Demoralizes foes when you roll for initiative
- **Terrified Retreat FEAT 7** (Master) - Cause foes you Demoralize to flee

## Level 15 Feats
- **Scare to Death FEAT 15** (Legendary) - Scare a target so much, they might die
