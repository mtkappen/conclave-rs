# Adventuring Gear

Adventuring gear includes equipment that helps characters survive in dangerous environments, explore dungeons, and overcome obstacles. This section covers essential items for adventurers.

## Backpacks and Containers

- **Backpack:** A leather pack for carrying goods (holds up to 30 lbs)
- **Bag of Holding:** Portable extradimensional space (holds up to 500 lbs, 64 cubic feet)
- **Chest:** Wooden container for storing items
- **Sack:** Simple cloth bag for carrying items

## Climbing and Mountaineering

- **Climber's Kit:** Includes pitons, crampons, and other climbing equipment
- **Grappling Hook:** Used with rope to scale walls or cliffs
- **Iron Spikes (Tent Pegs):** Used to secure tents or anchor ropes
- **Rope (Hemp):** 50 feet of hemp rope (holds 200 lbs)
- **Rope (Silk):** 50 feet of silk rope (holds 300 lbs, quieter than hemp)

## Light Sources

- **Candle:** Burns for 1 hour, provides dim light in a 5-foot radius
- **Lamp:** Burns oil, provides normal light in a 15-foot radius and dim light for 30 feet
- **Torch:** Burns for 1 hour, provides normal light in a 20-foot radius and dim light for 20 more feet
- **Hourglass:** Measures the passage of time
- **Sunrod:** Glows brightly for 6 hours when activated

## Survival Equipment

### Food and Water
- **Rations (1 day):** Dried food suitable for travel
- **Water Skin:** Holds 4 pounds of water
- **Waterskin (large):** Holds more water for extended travel

### Shelter
- **Tent (2-person):** Basic shelter from the elements
- **Tent (4-person):** Larger shelter for a group
- **Blanket:** Provides warmth in cold conditions

### Tools and Equipment
- **Crowbar:** Provides +5 to Strength checks when prying things open
- **Grappling Hook:** Used with rope for climbing or retrieval
- **Hammer:** For driving stakes or breaking objects
- **Pick:** For mining or breaking through obstacles
- **Shovel:** For digging trenches or graves

## Specialized Equipment

### Exploration Tools
- **Mirror (Steel):** Useful for signaling and seeing around corners
- **Magnifying Glass:** Allows closer inspection of small details
- **Net (3x5 ft.):** Can restrain creatures when thrown
- **Spike (Iron):** Used for climbing or securing objects

### Traps and Hazards
- **Caltrops:** Small spiked devices scattered on the ground (each creature moving through the area must make a Dexterity saving throw)
- **Ball Bearings:** Scattered to create difficult terrain
- **Manacles:** Restrain creatures
- **Poison, Basic:** Simple poison that can be applied to weapons

## Adventuring Packs

Pre-assembled packs provide common combinations of gear:

### Explorer's Pack
Includes: Backpack, bedroll, mess kit, tinderbox, 10 torches, 10 days of rations, waterskin, and 50 feet of rope.

### Dungeoneer's Pack
Includes: Backpack, crowbar, hammer, 10 pitons, 10 torches, tinderbox, 10 days of rations, waterskin, and 50 feet of hemp rope.

### Entertainer's Pack
Includes: Backpack, bedroll, costume, perfume, and 50 feet of silk rope.

### Priest's Pack
Includes: Backpack, blanket, candle, tinderbox, 10 torches, 10 days of rations, waterskin, and vestments.

## Currency

- **Gold Piece (gp):** Standard currency for expensive items
- **Silver Piece (sp):** Common currency for everyday transactions
- **Copper Piece (cp):** Small denomination for cheap items
- **Platinum Piece (pp):** High-value currency (1 pp = 10 gp)

### Exchange Rates
- 1 platinum piece = 10 gold pieces
- 1 gold piece = 10 silver pieces
- 1 silver piece = 10 copper pieces
