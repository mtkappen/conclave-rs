# Apocalypse

**Level:** 10  
**Traditions:** Arcane, Primal  
**Cast:** [three-actions] material, somatic, verbal  
**Range:** 1,000 feet; Area: 200-foot burst  
**Saving Throw:** Reflex  

You call down a cataclysmic event that devastates the area. Choose one of the following effects:

- **Meteor Storm:** Four meteors strike the area, each dealing 10d10 bludgeoning damage in a 30-foot burst (basic Reflex save).

- **Eternal Flame:** A massive firestorm engulfs the area, dealing 20d10 fire damage to all creatures (basic Reflex save).
- **Worldquake:** The ground splits and shakes, dealing 15d10 bludgeoning damage and creating fissures that are difficult terrain (basic Fortitude save).

Each creature in the area must attempt a saving throw. On a critical failure, the creature is also subject to additional effects determined by the GM (such as being buried under rubble or consumed by flames).
**Heightened (+1)** The damage increases by 2d10.
