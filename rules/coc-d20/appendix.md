## Glossary

### A

**Ability Check**: Rolling 1d20 + ability modifier when no skill applies  
**Abilities**: Six character attributes (Str, Dex, Con, Int, Wis, Cha)  
**AC (Armor Class)**: Difficulty to hit a character in combat  
**Advancement**: Increasing a creature's Hit Dice and power  
**Artifact**: Physical object imbued with Mythos power  
**Attack Roll**: Rolling 1d20 + bonuses to determine if attack hits  

### B

**Base Attack Bonus (BAB)**: Attack bonus from character level  
**Base Save Bonus**: Saving throw bonus from character level  
**Bluff**: Skill for lying and deception  
**Bonus**: Positive modifier to a roll  

### C

**Challenge Rating (CR)**: Measure of encounter difficulty  
**Charisma (Cha)**: Ability measuring force of personality  
**Concentration**: Skill for maintaining spells despite distraction  
**Constitution (Con)**: Ability measuring health and stamina  
**Critical Hit**: Attack that deals multiplied damage  
**Cthulhu Mythos Skill**: Knowledge of forbidden cosmic truths  

### D

**Damage Reduction (DR)**: Ignore first X points of damage  
**Dexterity (Dex)**: Ability measuring agility and reflexes  
**Difficulty Class (DC)**: Number to meet or beat on a check  
**Disable Device**: Skill for tampering with mechanical devices  
**Dodge Bonus**: Type of AC bonus that stacks  

### E

**Exotic Weapon**: Weapon requiring special training  
**Experience Points (XP)**: Currency for gaining levels  

### F

**Feat**: Special ability or training character possesses  
**Fortitude Save**: Saving throw against physical threats  
**Full Attack**: Full-round action allowing multiple attacks  

### G

**Grapple**: Special melee attack to restrain opponent  

### H

**Hit Dice (HD)**: Dice rolled for hit points  
**Hit Points (HP)**: Measure of health  

### I

**Intelligence (Int)**: Ability measuring learning and reasoning  
**Initiative**: Roll determining order in combat  
**Innate Ability**: Power possessed naturally, not learned  

### K

**Knowledge**: Skill covering academic subjects  

### L

**Level**: Measure of character power/experience  

### M

**Magic Item**: Object with magical properties  
**Melee Attack**: Close-combat attack  
**Mind-Affecting**: Effects that target the mind/psyche  

### N

**Nonplayer Character (NPC)**: Character controlled by GM  

### O

**Offense Option**: Character creation choice favoring attacks  
**Opposed Check**: Skill check against another's check result  

### P

**Penalty**: Negative modifier to a roll  
**Profession Template**: Starting skill selection for character  
**Psychic Feat**: Mental ability feat  

### R

**Ranged Attack**: Attack at distance  
**Reflex Save**: Saving throw against dodgable threats  
**Retries**: Attempting a skill check again after failure  

### S

**Sanity**: Measure of mental stability (unique to CoC)  
**Saving Throw**: Roll to resist harmful effects  
**Skill Check**: Roll using skill ranks + ability modifier  
**Spell**: Magical effect from Mythos knowledge  
**Strength (Str)**: Ability measuring physical power  
**Surprise**: Condition at start of combat  

### T

**Temporary Insanity**: Short-term mental breakdown  
**Touch Attack**: Attack against touch AC (ignores armor)  

### U

**Untrained Check**: Skill check with no ranks in skill  

### V

**Will Save**: Saving throw against mental threats  

### W

**Wisdom (Wis)**: Ability measuring willpower and perception

## Quick Reference

### Initiative Order
1. Determine surprise
2. Roll initiative (1d20 + Dex)
3. Act in descending order

### Attack Roll Formula
```
Attack Roll = 1d20 + BAB + Ability Mod + Size Mod + Other Mods
```

### Damage Roll Formula
```
Damage = Weapon Dice + Ability Mod + Other Mods
```

### AC Formula
```
AC = 10 + Armor + Shield + Dex + Size + Natural + Other
```

### Skill Check Formula
```
Skill Check = 1d20 + Ranks + Ability Mod + Circumstance Mods
```

### Sanity Loss (General)
- First encounter: Full loss
- Repeated similar: 1 point temporary only
