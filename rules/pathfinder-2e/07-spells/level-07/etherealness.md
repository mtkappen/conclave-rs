# Etherealness

**Level:** 7  
**Traditions:** Arcane, Occult  
**Cast:** [two-actions] somatic, verbal  
**Range:** touch; Targets: you and up to 5 willing creatures touched  
**Duration:** sustained up to 10 minutes  

You and the targets shift partially into the Ethereal Plane. While ethereal, you can see and hear on the Material Plane within 60 feet, but everything appears gray and hazy, and creatures and objects are concealed to you. You can move in any direction, including up and down, at half your normal Speed.

You can affect and be affected only by other creatures and effects that are also ethereal or that have the ethereal trait. Material Plane effects don't affect you, and you don't affect them. When the spell ends, you return to the Material Plane. If you're in the air, you fall (unless you can fly), and if you're inside an object, you're pushed into the nearest open space and take 1d6 damage per 5 feet you were pushed.
**Heightened (9th)** You can target up to five additional willing creatures at a range of 30 feet. The duration is up to 1 hour.
