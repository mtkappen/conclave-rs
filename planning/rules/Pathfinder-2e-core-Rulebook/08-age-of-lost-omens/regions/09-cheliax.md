# Cheliax

At its height, the empire of Cheliax had holdings that stretched across much of Avistan. After the Hellknights emerged as victors in the Hellknight Wars several decades ago, House Thrune did little to change Cheliax's direction.

Under House Thrune's rule, Cheliax transformed into a nation that viewed Hell as an ally. Diabolism became Cheliax's official state religion, and diabolic pacts maintain its power and keep order—the nation's internal security is legendary.

Many of Cheliax's outlying provinces have broken away, including Vidrian which gained independence after a devastating nautical loss in the Shackles resulted in Vidrian's independence.

Key features:
- Infernal empire allied with Hell
- Ruled by House Thrune
- Diabolism as state religion
- Hellknight order maintains strict law and order
- Former provinces seeking independence
