# Incendiary Cloud

**Level:** 9  
**Traditions:** Arcane, Primal  
**Cast:** [three-actions] material, somatic, verbal  
**Range:** 120 feet; Area: 20-foot burst  
**Duration:** sustained up to 1 minute  

You create a roiling cloud of flame that deals fire damage to creatures in the area. Each creature that starts its turn in the cloud or enters it for the first time on a turn must attempt a Reflex save.

- **Critical Success** The creature is unaffected.
- **Success** The creature takes half damage.
- **Failure** The creature takes full damage and is dazzled by the smoke.
- **Critical Failure** The creature takes double damage, is dazzled, and is also blinded for 1 round.

The cloud moves 10 feet away from you each round as a gust of wind disperses it slightly. You can Dismiss the spell at any time.

**Heightened (+1)** The damage increases by 2d8.
