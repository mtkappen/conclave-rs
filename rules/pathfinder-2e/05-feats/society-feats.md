# Society Skill Feats

## Level 1 Feats

### Courtly Graces FEAT 1
**Prerequisites:** trained in Society  
Use Society to get along in noble society; gain bonuses to Diplomacy checks in noble settings.

### Multilingual FEAT 1
**Prerequisites:** trained in Society  
Learn two new languages.

### Read Lips FEAT 1
**Prerequisites:** trained in Society  
Read the lips of people you can see; understand speech without hearing it.

### Sign Language FEAT 1
**Prerequisites:** trained in Society  
Learn sign languages; communicate silently with those who know sign language.

### Streetwise FEAT 1
**Prerequisites:** trained in Society  
Use Society to Gather Information and Recall Knowledge about criminal underworld or street life.

## Level 2 Feats

### Connections FEAT 2
**Prerequisites:** expert in Society, Courtly Graces  
Leverage your connections for favors and meetings; gain bonuses when requesting assistance from contacts.

## Level 15 Feats

### Legendary Codebreaker FEAT 15
**Prerequisites:** legendary in Society  
Quickly Decipher Writing using Society; reduce time required by half.

### Legendary Linguist FEAT 15
**Prerequisites:** legendary in Society, Multilingual  
Create pidgin languages to communicate with anyone; combine known languages to create makeshift communication.
