# Harrison Armory (HA)

HA mechs are the most advanced machines in the business, equipped with powerful reactors that provide excellent heat management and enable the use of high-tech energy weapons.

Harrison Armory is one of the oldest and most prestigious mech manufacturers in the galaxy, hailing from Cradle itself – the heart of Union and humanity. HA's designs reflect the values of their home world: discipline, precision, and overwhelming firepower. Their mechs are known for superior heat management systems, allowing them to sustain prolonged combat engagements that would cripple other frames. HA pilots are typically disciplined professionals who value efficiency and tactical superiority over flashiness.

## HA Core Bonuses
HA bonuses enhance your mech's heat management, energy weapon effectiveness, and sustained combat capabilities. You must have at least three license levels in HA licenses to take a bonus from this list.

### Heat Sink Optimization
Your HEAT CAP increases by 2. Additionally, when you clear heat through any means, you may clear 2 additional heat.

### Energy Weapon Enhancement
Your energy weapons gain +1� on attack rolls and deal +1d3 bonus damage on hits.

### Overclocked Reactor
Once per mission, you may use your CORE SYSTEM twice. Additionally, your mech gains +2 SP.
