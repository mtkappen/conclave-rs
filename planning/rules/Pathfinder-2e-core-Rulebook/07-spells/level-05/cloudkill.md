# CLOUDKILL

## Spell 5 - Death Necromancy Poison

**Traditions:** arcane, primal  
**Cast:** [three-actions] material, somatic, verbal  
**Range:** 120 feet; **Area:** 20-foot burst  
**Saving Throw:** basic Fortitude; **Duration:** 1 minute  

You conjure a poisonous fog. This functions as obscuring mist (page 355), except the area moves 10 feet away from you each round. You deal 6d8 poison damage to each breathing creature that starts its turn in the spell's area. You can Dismiss the spell.

**Heightened (+1):** The damage increases by 1d8.
