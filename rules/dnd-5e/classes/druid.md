# Druid

You are a guardian of nature, drawing power from the primal forces of the natural world. Druids can cast spells, transform into animals, and protect the balance of nature.

## Core Druid Traits

- **Primary Ability:** Wisdom
- **Hit Point Die:** D8 per Druid level
- **Saving Throws:** Intelligence and Wisdom

### Proficiencies
- **Skill Proficiencies:** Choose 2: Animal Handling, Arcana, Insight, Medicine, Nature, Perception, Religion, or Survival
- **Weapon Proficiencies:** Simple weapons
- **Tool Proficiencies:** Herbalism Kit
- **Armor Training:** Light armor and Shields (non-metal)

### Starting Equipment
Choose A or B:
- (A) Leather Armor, Shield, Sickle, Druidic Focus (Quarterstaff), Explorer's Pack, Herbalism Kit, and 9 GP
- (B) 50 GP

## Class Features

### Level 1: Spellcasting
You have learned to cast spells through studying the mystical forces of nature. Wisdom is your spellcasting ability for Druid spells. You can use a Druidic Focus as a Spellcasting Focus.

- **Cantrips:** You know two cantrips of your choice from the Druid spell list
- **Spells:** You prepare level 1+ spells from the Druid spell list (number equals Wisdom modifier + Druid level)
- **Spell Slots:** The Druid Features table shows how many spell slots you have

### Level 1: Druidic
You know Druidic, the secret language of Druids. You can use it to leave hidden messages that others automatically spot if they know Druidic. Others spot the message's presence with a successful DC 15 Intelligence (Investigation) check but can't decipher it without magic.

### Level 1: Primal Order
You have dedicated yourself to one of the following sacred roles:
- **Trained for battle:** Gain proficiency with Martial weapons and training with Medium armor
- **Mystical connection:** Know one extra cantrip and gain a bonus to Intelligence (Arcana or Nature) checks equal to your Wisdom modifier

### Level 2: Wild Shape
The power of nature allows you to assume the form of an animal. As a Bonus Action, shape-shift into a Beast form you have learned for this feature. You stay in that form for a number of hours equal to half your Druid level or until you use Wild Shape again, have the Incapacitated condition, or die.

- **Uses:** You can use Wild Shape twice. Regain one use on a Short Rest, all uses on a Long Rest
- **Known Forms:** You know four Beast forms with CR 1/4 or lower that lack a Fly Speed (at level 2)
- **Rules While Shape-Shifted:** You retain your personality, memories, ability to speak, Hit Points, and class features. Your game statistics are replaced by the Beast's stat block

### Level 2: Wild Companion
You can summon a nature spirit that assumes an animal familiar to aid you. As a Magic action, expend a spell slot or use of Wild Shape to cast Find Familiar without Material components. The familiar is Fey and disappears when you finish a Long Rest.

### Level 3: Druid Subclass
Choose a subclass (Circle). The Circle of the Land is detailed in the SRD.

### Level 4: Nature's Magic
Your connection to nature gives you additional magical capabilities based on your subclass choice.

### Level 5: Wild Resurgence
Once on each of your turns, if you have no uses of Wild Shape left, you can give yourself one use by expending a spell slot (no action required). You can also expend one use of Wild Shape to give yourself a level 1 spell slot.

### Level 7: Elemental Fury
Choose one option:
- **Primal Strike:** Add your Wisdom modifier to the damage you deal with any Druid cantrip
- Once on each of your turns when you hit with an attack using a weapon or Beast form's attack in Wild Shape, cause the target to take an extra 1d8 Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Thunder damage

### Level 10: Nature's Ward
You are immune to the Poisoned condition and have Resistance to a damage type associated with your land choice.

### Level 14: Nature's Sanctuary
As a Magic action, expend a use of Wild Shape to cause spectral trees and vines to appear in a 15-foot Cube within 120 feet. You and allies have Half Cover while in that area and gain Resistance from Nature's Ward. As a Bonus Action, you can move the Cube up to 60 feet.

### Level 18: Beast Spells
While using Wild Shape, you can cast spells in Beast form, except for any spell that has a Material component with a cost specified or that consumes its Material component.

### Level 20: Archdruid
The vitality of Nature constantly blooms within you:
- Whenever you roll Initiative and have no uses of Wild Shape left, you regain one expended use
- You can convert uses of Wild Shape into spell slots (each use contributes 2 spell levels)
- The primal magic causes you to age more slowly (10 years pass for every 1 year of aging)

## Circle of the Land (Subclass)

The Circle of the Land comprises mystics and sages who safeguard ancient knowledge and rites within sacred circles of trees or standing stones.

### Level 3: Land's Aid
As a Magic action, expend a use of Wild Shape and choose a point within 60 feet. Flowers and thorns appear in a 10-foot-radius Sphere. Creatures make Constitution saves, taking Necrotic damage on failure or half on success. One creature regains Hit Points equal to the damage dealt.

### Level 3: Circle of the Land Spells
Choose one type of land (arid, polar, temperate, or tropical). You always have the listed spells prepared for your Druid level.

### Level 6: Natural Recovery
You can cast one prepared spell without expending a spell slot after a Long Rest. After a Short Rest, you can recover spell slots with a combined level equal to or less than half your Druid level (round up), none of which can be level 6+.
