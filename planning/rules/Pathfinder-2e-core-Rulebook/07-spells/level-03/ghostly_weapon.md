# GHOSTLY WEAPON

## Spell 3 - Transmutation

**Traditions:** arcane, occult  
**Cast:** [two-actions] somatic, verbal  
**Range:** touch; **Targets:** 1 non-magical weapon that is either unattended or wielded by you or a willing ally  
**Duration:** 5 minutes  

The target becomes a ghostly weapon. It gains the ghost trait and can affect incorporeal creatures as if they were corporeal. The weapon also gains a +1 status bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls.
