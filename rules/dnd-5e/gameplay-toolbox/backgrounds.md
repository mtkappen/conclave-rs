# Backgrounds

A character's background represents the place and occupation that were most formative for them before becoming an adventurer. Backgrounds provide skill proficiencies, tool proficiencies, languages, equipment, and a unique feature.

## Background Benefits

Each background provides:
- **Skill Proficiencies:** Two skills of your choice (or specific skills)
- **Tool Proficiencies:** One type of tool or language(s)
- **Equipment:** Starting gear appropriate to the background
- **Feature:** A unique benefit that reflects your background
- **Suggested Characteristics:** Personality traits, ideals, bonds, and flaws

## Common Backgrounds

### Acolyte
You have spent much of your life in a temple, serving a deity or pantheon.

**Skill Proficiencies:** Insight, Religion  
**Tool Proficiencies:** One type of gaming set or musical instrument  
**Languages:** Two of your choice  
**Equipment:** Holy symbol, prayer book or wheel, 5 sticks of incense, vestments, common clothes, pouch with 15 gp  

**Feature: Shelter of the Faithful**
As a member of a religious order, you can find shelter and aid from fellow believers. You can receive free healing and care at temples, shrines, or other holy sites.

### Criminal
You have a history of breaking the law and have spent time in prison or on the run.

**Skill Proficiencies:** Deception, Stealth  
**Tool Proficiencies:** One type of gaming set, thieves' tools  
**Equipment:** Crowbar, dark common clothes including a hood, pouch with 15 gp  

**Feature: Criminal Contact**
You have a reliable and trustworthy contact who acts as your liaison to a criminal network.

### Entertainer
You thrive in front of an audience and know how to captivate them.

**Skill Proficiencies:** Acrobatics, Performance  
**Tool Proficiencies:** Disguise kit, one type of musical instrument  
**Equipment:** Instrument (one of your choice), favorite costume, letter of introduction, pouch with 15 gp  

**Feature: By Popular Demand**
You can always find a place to perform in any town or city where you have access to an audience.

### Folk Hero
You come from humble beginnings and are seen as a defender of the common people.

**Skill Proficiencies:** Animal Handling, Survival  
**Tool Proficiencies:** One type of artisan's tools, vehicles (land)  
**Equipment:** Shovel, iron pot, common clothes, pouch with 10 gp  

**Feature: Rustic Hospitality**
Since you come from the ranks of the common folk, you fit in among them with ease. You can find a place to hide, rest, or recuperate among other commoners.

### Noble
You understand wealth, power, and privilege. You have a noble title and family lineage.

**Skill Proficiencies:** History, Persuasion  
**Tool Proficiencies:** One type of gaming set  
**Languages:** One of your choice  
**Equipment:** Fine clothes, signet ring, scroll of pedigree, pouch with 25 gp  

**Feature: Position of Privilege**
Thanks to your noble birth, people are inclined to think the best of you. You are welcome in high society, and people assume you have the right to be wherever you are.

### Sage
You spent years learning the lore of the multiverse, studying at universities or libraries.

**Skill Proficiencies:** Arcana, History  
**Languages:** Two of your choice  
**Equipment:** Ink (1 ounce bottle), quill, small knife, letter from a dead colleague, common clothes, pouch with 10 gp  

**Feature: Researcher**
When attempting to learn or recall a piece of lore, if you do not know that information, you often know where and from whom you can obtain it.

### Soldier
You have served in a military unit, participating in battles and campaigns.

**Skill Proficiencies:** Athletics, Intimidation  
**Tool Proficiencies:** One type of gaming set, vehicles (land)  
**Equipment:** Insignia of rank, trophy taken from fallen enemy, common clothes, pouch with 10 gp  

**Feature: Military Rank**
You have a military rank from your career as a soldier. Soldiers loyal to your former military organization still recognize your authority and influence.

### Urchin
You grew up on the streets alone, orphaned, and poor. You had no one to watch over you or provide for you.

**Skill Proficiencies:** Sleight of Hand, Stealth  
**Tool Proficiencies:** Disguise kit, thieves' tools  
**Equipment:** Small knife, map of your city, pet mouse, token from a deceased parent, common clothes, pouch with 10 gp  

**Feature: City Secrets**
You know the secret patterns and flow to cities and can find passages through urban environments that others would miss. You travel at normal pace even when moving stealthily in urban environments.

## Customizing Backgrounds

If none of the standard backgrounds fit your character concept, you can create a custom background:

1. **Choose two skill proficiencies** from any skills
2. **Choose one tool proficiency** or two languages
3. **Choose equipment** totaling no more than 25 gp in value
4. **Create a feature** that reflects your background (work with your DM)
5. **Develop suggested characteristics** based on your story

## Background and Class Combination

Your background and class work together to create a unique character:
- Your background represents who you were before adventuring
- Your class represents what you do now as an adventurer
- The combination can tell an interesting story (e.g., a Noble Wizard, a Criminal Rogue, a Soldier Fighter)
