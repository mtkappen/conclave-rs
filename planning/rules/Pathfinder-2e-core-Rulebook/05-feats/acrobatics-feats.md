# Acrobatics Skill Feats

## Level 1 Feats
- **Cat Fall FEAT 1** - Treat falls as shorter than they are
- **Quick Squeeze FEAT 1** - Move swiftly as you Squeeze  
- **Steady Balance FEAT 1** - Maintain your balance in adverse conditions

## Level 2 Feats
- **Nimble Crawl FEAT 2** (Expert) - Crawl at a faster rate

## Level 7 Feats
- **Kip Up FEAT 7** (Master) - Stand up for free without triggering reactions
