# Chapter 3: Feats

Feats represent special training or abilities that characters possess. Each character starts with two feats at 1st level and gains another feat at every level divisible by three (3rd, 6th, 9th, 12th, 15th, and 18th).

## Acquiring Feats

When you gain a feat, choose from the general feats listed below. Some feats have prerequisites that your character must meet before selecting them.

**Note**: If you don't meet a feat's prerequisites when you select it, you lose the benefit of the feat until you qualify for it again.

## General Feats

### Ambidexterity
**Prerequisites**: Dexterity 15+  
**Benefit**: You suffer no penalties for fighting with two weapons. Normally, using a weapon in your off-hand imposes a -6 penalty on attack rolls with that weapon and a -4 penalty on attack rolls with your primary hand. With this feat, these penalties are eliminated.

### Animal Empathy
**Prerequisites**: Handle Animal skill  
**Benefit**: You gain a +2 synergy bonus on Handle Animal checks. Additionally, you can use Animal Empathy to improve an animal's attitude as a full-round action.

### Ambush
**Prerequisites**: Hide, Move Silently  
**Benefit**: When you successfully surprise an opponent, you gain a +2 bonus on your attack roll.

### Athletic
**Prerequisites**: Str 13+  
**Benefit**: You gain a +2 bonus on Climb, Jump, and Swim checks.

### Blind-Fight
**Prerequisites**: Base Attack Bonus +1  
**Benefit**: In melee combat against an invisible opponent, you lose your Dexterity bonus to AC (if any), but you still get to make attacks. If you hit, there's a 20% miss chance. You can reroll the miss chance once per attack.

### Cautious
**Prerequisites**: Wis 13+  
**Benefit**: You gain a +2 bonus on all saving throws against traps and illusions.

### Combat Casting
**Prerequisites**: Concentration skill  
**Benefit**: You gain a +4 bonus on Concentration checks made to cast spells while grappled or when casting defensively.

### Combat Reflexes
**Prerequisites**: Dexterity 13+  
**Benefit**: You can make additional attacks of opportunity per round equal to your Dexterity modifier. You can also make attacks of opportunity while flat-footed.

### Deft Hands
**Prerequisites**: Dex 15+  
**Benefit**: You gain a +2 bonus on Disable Device, Open Lock, and Sleight of Hand checks.

### Diehard
**Prerequisites**: Constitution 13+  
**Benefit**: When your hit points drop below 0 but you're not dead, you're automatically stabilized. You can also act normally while disabled (at negative hit points).

### Dodge
**Prerequisites**: Dexterity 13+, Combat Reflexes  
**Benefit**: You gain a +1 dodge bonus to your Armor Class. This bonus applies even against touch attacks or if you're flat-footed.

### Endurance
**Prerequisites**: Constitution 13+  
**Benefit**: You gain a +4 bonus on checks and saves for:
- Running for long periods
- Holding your breath
- Avoiding nonlethal damage from extreme temperatures
- Marching without rest

### Extra Turning
**Prerequisites**: Ability to turn undead  
**Benefit**: You can turn undead four more times per day than normal.

### Fast Metabolism
**Prerequisites**: Constitution 15+  
**Benefit**: You heal double the normal amount of damage from natural healing. You also gain a +2 bonus on saves against poison and disease.

### Great Fortitude
**Prerequisites**: Constitution 13+  
**Benefit**: You gain a +2 bonus on all Fortitude saving throws.

### Improved Bull Rush
**Prerequisites**: Str 13+, Base Attack Bonus +1  
**Benefit**: You don't provoke an attack of opportunity when making a bull rush attempt. You also gain a +4 bonus on checks made to bull rush.

### Improved Disarm
**Prerequisites**: Base Attack Bonus +1  
**Benefit**: You don't provoke an attack of opportunity when attempting to disarm an opponent. You also gain a +4 bonus on checks made to disarm.

### Improved Initiative
**Prerequisites**: -  
**Benefit**: You gain a +4 bonus on initiative checks.

### Improved Grapple
**Prerequisites**: Base Attack Bonus +1  
**Benefit**: You don't provoke an attack of opportunity when making a grapple attempt. You also gain a +4 bonus on grapple checks.

### Improved Sunder
**Prerequisites**: Str 13+, Base Attack Bonus +1  
**Benefit**: You don't provoke an attack of opportunity when attempting to sunder an opponent's weapon or armor. You also gain a +4 bonus on checks made to sunder.

### Iron Will
**Prerequisites**: Wisdom 13+  
**Benefit**: You gain a +2 bonus on all Will saving throws.

### Lightning Reflexes
**Prerequisites**: Dexterity 13+  
**Benefit**: You gain a +2 bonus on all Reflex saving throws.

### Multiattack
**Prerequisites**: Base Attack Bonus +6, three or more natural attacks  
**Benefit**: Your secondary natural attacks suffer only a -2 penalty instead of the normal -5.

### Negotiator
**Prerequisites**: Diplomacy, Gather Information  
**Benefit**: You gain a +2 bonus on Diplomacy and Gather Information checks.

### Nimble Fingers
**Prerequisites**: Dexterity 15+  
**Benefit**: You gain a +2 bonus on Disable Device and Open Lock checks.

### Persuasive
**Prerequisites**: Charisma 13+  
**Benefit**: You gain a +2 bonus on Bluff, Diplomacy, and Intimidate checks.

### Point Blank Shot
**Prerequisites**: Base Attack Bonus +1  
**Benefit**: You get a +1 bonus on attack and damage rolls with ranged weapons at ranges of up to 30 feet.

### Power Attack
**Prerequisites**: Strength 13+, Base Attack Bonus +1  
**Benefit**: On your action, before making attack rolls for a round, you may choose to subtract a number from all melee attack rolls and add the same number to all melee damage rolls. The maximum penalty is equal to your base attack bonus.

### Quick Draw
**Prerequisites**: Base Attack Bonus +1  
**Benefit**: You can draw a weapon as a free action instead of a move action. You can throw weapons at your full normal rate of attacks.

### Rapid Shot
**Prerequisites**: Dexterity 13+, Base Attack Bonus +1  
**Benefit**: You can fire one additional time with a ranged weapon this round, but all attack rolls suffer a -2 penalty.

### Run
**Prerequisites**: -  
**Benefit**: When running, you move five times your speed in a straight line (instead of four). You maintain your Dexterity bonus to AC while running.

### Self-Sufficient
**Prerequisites**: Heal, Survival/Wilderness Lore  
**Benefit**: You gain a +2 bonus on Heal and Wilderness Lore checks.

### Silent Spell
**Prerequisites**: Caster level 3rd+  
**Benefit**: You can cast spells without verbal components. Spells modified by this feat take up a spell slot one level higher than normal.

### Steady Aim
**Prerequisites**: Base Attack Bonus +4, Point Blank Shot  
**Benefit**: If you spend your entire turn aiming at a target with a ranged weapon, you gain a +2 bonus on the attack roll and damage roll.

### Stealthy
**Prerequisites**: Hide, Move Silently  
**Benefit**: You gain a +2 bonus on Hide and Move Silently checks.

### Track
**Prerequisites**: Wisdom 13+  
**Benefit**: You can follow tracks through the wilderness. The DC depends on the surface and conditions.

### Two-Weapon Fighting
**Prerequisites**: Dexterity 15+, Base Attack Bonus +1  
**Benefit**: If you wield a second weapon in your off hand, you reduce the penalty for attacking with both weapons from -6/-4 to -4/-2.

### Weapon Finesse
**Prerequisites**: Base Attack Bonus +1  
**Benefit**: With a light weapon, rapier, whip, or spiked chain made for a creature of your size category, you may use your Dexterity modifier instead of your Strength modifier on attack rolls.

### Weapon Focus (weapon)
**Prerequisites**: Base Attack Bonus +1  
**Benefit**: You gain a +1 bonus on all attack rolls made with the selected weapon.

### Weapon Specialization (weapon)
**Prerequisites**: Weapon Focus (that weapon), Fighter level 4th+  
**Benefit**: You gain a +2 bonus on damage rolls made with the selected weapon.

## Psychic Feats

Psychic feats represent mental abilities that characters can develop through training and discipline. These are particularly useful for investigators dealing with supernatural threats.

### Concentrated Mind
**Prerequisites**: Concentration 5+ ranks  
**Benefit**: You gain a +2 bonus on Concentration checks. If you have 10+ ranks in Concentration, this bonus increases to +4.

### Mental Barrier
**Prerequisites**: Wisdom 13+, Psychic Focus  
**Benefit**: You gain a +2 bonus on Will saving throws against mind-affecting effects. This bonus increases to +4 if you succeed on a DC 15 Psychic Focus check before making the save.

### Mind Over Matter
**Prerequisites**: Constitution 15+, Iron Will  
**Benefit**: Once per day, when you fail a Fortitude or Will saving throw, you can reroll the save. You must take the second result.

### Psychic Resistance
**Prerequisites**: Wisdom 15+, Mental Barrier  
**Benefit**: You gain a +4 bonus on Will saves against psychic attacks and spells from creatures with the psychic subtype.

### Telepathic Bond
**Prerequisites**: Caster level 5th+  
**Benefit**: You can form a telepathic link with one or more willing creatures within 30 feet, allowing silent communication.

## Feat Descriptions

Each feat has specific rules for when and how it can be used:

- **Prerequisites**: Requirements that must be met before selecting the feat
- **Benefit**: What the feat does for your character
- **Normal**: What happens without the feat (if applicable)
- **Special**: Additional information about the feat
