# Thievery Skill Feats

## Level 1 Feats
- **Pickpocket FEAT 1** - Steal or Palm an Object more effectively
- **Subtle Theft FEAT 1** - Your thefts are harder to notice

## Level 2 Feats
- **Wary Disarmament FEAT 2** (Expert) - +2 to AC or saves against devices or traps you trigger while disarming

## Level 7 Feats
- **Quick Unlock FEAT 7** (Master) - Pick a Lock with 1 action

## Level 15 Feats
- **Legendary Thief FEAT 15** (Legendary, Pickpocket) - Steal what would normally be impossible to steal
