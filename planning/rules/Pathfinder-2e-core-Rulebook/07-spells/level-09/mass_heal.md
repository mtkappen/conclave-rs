# Mass Heal

**Level:** 9  
**Traditions:** Divine, Primal  
**Cast:** [three-actions] material, somatic, verbal  
**Range:** 120 feet; Area: 60-foot burst  
**Duration:** instantaneous  

You channel powerful positive energy to heal all living creatures in the area. Each living creature regains 15d8 Hit Points and is cured of one disease or poison affecting it. Undead creatures in the area take 15d8 positive damage (basic Reflex save).

Additionally, you can choose to remove one of the following conditions from each living creature in the area: blinded, deafened, paralyzed, or stunned.

**Heightened (+1)** The healing and damage increase by 2d8.
