# Disrupt Undead

**Level:** Cantrip
**Traditions:** divine, primal  
**Cast:** [two-actions] somatic, verbal  
**Range:** 30 feet; **Targets:** 1 undead creature  
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude  

You deal 1d4 positive damage to the target. The target must attempt a basic Fortitude save. On a critical failure, the target is also dazzled for 1 round.
**Heightened (+1):** The damage increases by 1d4.
