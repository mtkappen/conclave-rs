# Chapter 8: Creatures

This chapter details the monstrous entities of the Cthulhu Mythos that investigators may encounter. These creatures range from cultist minions to cosmic horrors beyond human comprehension.

## Reading the Entries

Each creature entry follows a standard format:

### Creature Statistics Block

```
CREATURE NAME
Size Type; HD Hit Dice; Init Initiative; Spd Speed
AC Armor Class (touch, flat-footed); hp Hit Points; Morale Morale Bonus
Base Attack/Grapple Base Attack Bonus/Grapple Bonus
Attack Melee or Ranged attack
Full Attack Full attack routine
Space/Reach Space in combat/Reach in combat
Special Attacks Special offensive abilities
Special Qualities Special defensive and other abilities
Saves Fortitude, Reflex, Will
Abilities Strength, Dexterity, Constitution, Intelligence, Wisdom, Charisma
Skills Skills with modifiers
Feats Feats possessed
Environment Environment where found
Organization Organization size
Challenge Rating CR
Treasure Treasure found
Alignment Typical alignment
Advancement Level range for advancement
```

### Entry Components

**Size and Type**: Creature's physical size and creature type (humanoid, monstrous, etc.)

**Hit Dice**: Determines hit points and base attack bonus/saves

**Initiative**: Dexterity modifier plus any bonuses

**Speed**: Movement rate in feet per round

**Armor Class**: Calculated AC with touch and flat-footed values

**Hit Points**: Current or average hit points

**Morale**: Bonus on morale checks; typically 0 for mindless creatures

**Base Attack/Grapple**: Base attack bonus and grapple modifier

**Attack**: Primary attack form with damage

**Full Attack**: Complete attack routine when using full-round action

**Space/Reach**: Square size occupied and reach in feet

**Special Attacks**: Unique offensive capabilities

**Special Qualities**: Defensive abilities, senses, and other traits

**Saves**: Fortitude, Reflex, and Will save bonuses

**Abilities**: Six ability scores

**Skills**: Skill modifiers with key abilities noted

**Feats**: Feats possessed by the creature

**Environment**: Where the creature is typically found

**Organization**: Typical group size (solitary, pair, gang, etc.)

**Challenge Rating**: Encounter difficulty relative to party level

**Treasure**: Standard treasure for creatures of this CR

**Alignment**: Typical moral and ethical alignment

**Advancement**: How the creature can be advanced with more HD

## Creature Feats

Creatures may possess feats similar to player characters. Common creature feats include:

### Natural Attack Feats

- **Multiattack**: Secondary natural attacks take only -2 penalty
- **Power Attack**: Trade attack bonus for damage with natural weapons
- **Improved Grapple**: Better at grappling opponents

### Combat Feats

- **Cleave**: Additional attack after dropping opponent
- **Great Cleave**: Multiple additional attacks after dropping opponents
- **Improved Bull Rush**: No AoO when bull rushing; +4 bonus
- **Improved Sunder**: No AoO when sundering; +4 bonus

### Special Creature Feats

- **Damage Reduction (DR)**: Ignore first X points of damage from each attack
- **Energy Resistance**: Resist specific energy types (fire, cold, electricity)
- **Regeneration**: Regain hit points each round unless damaged by specific source
- **Immunity**: Completely immune to certain effects
