# Enhance Victuals

## Spell 2 - Transmutation
**Traditions:** divine, primal  
**Cast:** 1 minute (material, somatic, verbal)  
**Range:** touch; **Targets:** up to 1 gallon of non-magical water or up to 5 pounds of food  

You transform the target into nourishing sustenance. The target becomes sufficient for one Medium creature's daily food and water requirements.
