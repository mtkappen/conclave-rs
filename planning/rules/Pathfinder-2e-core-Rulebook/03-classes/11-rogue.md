# Rogue

## Introduction

You are skilled and opportunistic. Using your sharp wits and quick reactions, you take advantage of your opponents' missteps and strike where it hurts most. You play a dangerous game, seeking thrills and testing your skills, and likely don't care much for any laws that happen to get in your way. While the path of every rogue is unique and riddled with danger, the one thing you all share in common is the breadth and depth of your skills.

### During combat encounters...
You move about stealthily so you can catch foes unawares. You're a precision instrument, more useful against a tough boss or distant spellcaster than against rank-and-file soldiers.

### During social encounters...
Your skills give you multiple tools to influence your opposition. Pulling cons and ferreting out information are second nature to you.

### While exploring...
You sneak to get the drop on foes and scout for danger or traps. You're a great asset, since you can disable traps, solve puzzles, and anticipate dangers.

### In downtime...
You might pick pockets or trade in illegal goods. You can also become part of a thieves' guild, or even found one of your own.

### You Might...
- Hone your skills through intense practice, both on your own and out in the world.
- Know where to attain illicit goods.
- Skirt or break the law because you think it's meaningless or have your own code.

### Others Probably...
- Find you charming or fascinating, even if they think they know better than to trust you.
- Come to you when they need someone who is willing to take risks or use questionable methods.
- Suspect you're motivated primarily by greed.

## Initial Proficiencies

| Statistic | Proficiency |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Key Ability** | Dexterity or Other (from racket) |
| **Hit Points** | 8 + your Constitution modifier |
| **Perception** | Expert in Perception |
| **Saving Throws** | Trained in Fortitude, Expert in Reflex, Expert in Will |
| **Skills** | Trained in Stealth, Trained in one or more skills determined by your rogue's racket, Trained in a number of additional skills equal to 7 plus your Intelligence modifier |

## Class Features

You gain these abilities as a rogue. Abilities gained at higher levels list the level at which you gain them next to the features' names.

### Ancestry and Background (1st)
In addition to what you get from your class at 1st level, you have the benefits of your selected ancestry and background, as described in Chapter 2.

### Rogue's Racket (1st)
As you started on the path of the rogue, you began to develop your own style to pursue your illicit activities. Your racket shapes your rogue techniques and the way you approach a job. Choose one:

**Ruffian:** You prefer to strong-arm or intimidate others rather than rely on finesse. You can deal sneak attack damage with any simple weapon. When you critically succeed at an attack roll using a simple weapon against a flat-footed target, you also apply the critical specialization effect. You're trained in Intimidation and medium armor. You can choose Strength as your key ability score.

**Scoundrel:** You use fast-talking, flattery, and a silver tongue to avoid danger. When you successfully Feint, the target is flat-footed against melee attacks from you until the end of your next turn. On a critical success, the target is flat-footed against all melee attacks until the end of your next turn. You're trained in Deception and Diplomacy. You can choose Charisma as your key ability score.

**Thief:** Nothing beats the thrill of taking something that belongs to someone else. When you attack with a finesse melee weapon, you can add your Dexterity modifier to damage rolls instead of Strength. You are trained in Thievery.

### Sneak Attack (1st)
When your enemy can't properly defend itself, you deal extra damage. If you Strike a flat-footed creature with an agile or finesse melee weapon, agile or finesse unarmed attack, or ranged weapon attack, you deal an extra 1d6 precision damage. Increase by one die at 5th, 11th, and 17th levels.

### Surprise Attack (1st)
On the first round of combat, if you roll Deception or Stealth for initiative, creatures that haven't acted are flat-footed to you.

### Rogue Feats (1st)
At 1st level and every even-numbered level, you gain a rogue class feat.

### Skill Feats (1st)
At 1st level and every level thereafter, you gain a skill feat from Chapter 5.

### Skill Increases (2nd)
At 2nd level and every level thereafter, you gain a skill increase. Use it to become trained in one skill or expert in one skill you're already trained in. At 7th level you can become master, and at 15th level legendary.

### Deny Advantage (3rd)
You aren't flat-footed to hidden, undetected, or flanking creatures of your level or lower.

### General Feats (3rd)
At 3rd level and every 4 levels thereafter, you gain a general feat from Chapter 5.

### Ability Boosts (5th)
At 5th level and every 5 levels thereafter, you boost four different ability scores.

### Ancestry Feats (5th)
You gain an ancestry feat at 5th level and every 4 levels thereafter.

### Weapon Tricks (5th)
You gain expert proficiency in simple weapons, rapier, sap, shortbow, and shortsword. When you critically succeed at an attack roll against a flat-footed target using one of these weapons, you apply the critical specialization effects.

### Evasion (7th)
Your proficiency rank for Reflex saves increases to master. On a success, you get a critical success instead.

### Vigilant Senses (7th)
Your proficiency rank for Perception increases to master.

### Great Fortitude (9th)
Your proficiency rank for Fortitude saves increases to expert. On a failure against damage, you take half damage instead of double.

### Debilitating Strike (9th)
When you deal sneak attack damage to a flat-footed target, you can apply one debilitation. Target attempts Fortitude save vs your class DC:
- **Clumsy Strike:** Target becomes clumsy 1
- **Confounding Strike:** Target is confused until end of your next turn  
- **Dazing Strike:** Target is dazed 1
- **Enfeebled Strike:** Target becomes enfeebled 1
- **Fascinating Strike:** Target is fascinated for 1 round
- **Fatiguing Strike:** Target becomes fatigued
- **Paralyzing Strike:** Target is paralyzed for 1 round
- **Sickening Strike:** Target becomes sickened 1
- **Slowing Strike:** Target is slowed 1

### Incredible Senses (13th)
Your proficiency rank for Perception increases to legendary.

### Light Armor Expertise (13th)
Your proficiency ranks for light armor and unarmored defense increase to expert.

### Improved Evasion (13th)
On a success on Reflex save against attack damage, you get critical success and take no damage. On a failure, you get success and take half damage.

### Master Tricks (13th)
Choose two master rogue talents: Acrobatic Tumble, Fast Stealth, Opportune Riposte, or Slippery Mind.

### Greater Weapon Specialization (15th)
Simple weapons, rapier, sap, shortbow, shortsword become master proficiency. Unarmed attacks become expert. Deal 3 extra damage with master weapons, 2 extra with expert/unarmed.

### Slippery Mind (17th)
Your proficiency rank for Will saves increases to master. On a success, you get critical success instead.

### Sneak Attack Increase (17th)
Sneak attack damage increases to 4d6 precision damage.

### Light Armor Mastery (19th)
Your proficiency ranks for light armor and unarmored defense increase to master.

### Master Strike (19th)
When you deal sneak attack damage, ignore all damage resistance the target has (not immunity).

## Rogue Feats by Level

### 1st Level Feats
- **Nimble Dodge:** When hit by an attack, gain +2 circumstance bonus to AC against that attack as a reaction.
- **Poison Weapon:** Apply simple poison to weapon as one action; on hit, poison deals 1d4 poison damage instead of requiring target to fail save first.

### 2nd Level Feats
- **Quick Draw:** Draw or sheathe two weapons when you would normally draw or sheathe one.
- **Skirmish Strike:** When you Stride at least 10 feet, make a melee Strike with +1 circumstance bonus to attack roll.

### 6th Level Feats
- **Sneak Savant:** Once per round when you deal sneak attack damage, gain temporary HP equal to half your rogue level.

### 8th Level Feats
- **Blind-Fight (Prereq: master Perception):** Don't need flat check to target concealed creatures; not flat-footed to hidden creatures; DC 5 flat check to target hidden creatures. While adjacent to undetected creature of your level or lower, it is only hidden from you.
- **Delay Trap (Reaction):** Trigger trap within reach is triggered. Attempt Thievery check to Disable with +5 DC. Critical Success: prevent trigger or delay to start/end of next turn. Success: prevent or delay to end of next turn. Failure: no effect. Critical Failure: flat-footed until start of next turn.
- **Improved Poison Weapon (Prereq: Poison Weapon):** Simple poison deals 2d4 poison damage instead of 1d4. Don't waste poison on critical failure attack.
- **Nimble Roll (Prereq: Nimble Dodge):** Use Nimble Dodge before Reflex save; if attack fails/crit fails or save succeeds/crit succeeds, Stride up to 10 feet as part of reaction. Can use while Flying/Swimming.
- **Opportune Backstab (Reaction):** Trigger creature within melee reach is hit by ally's melee attack. Make Strike against triggering creature.
- **Sidestep (Reaction):** Trigger attack targeting you fails or crit fails. Redirect attack to adjacent creature within reach of triggering attack; attacker rerolls against new target.
- **Sly Striker:** When succeeding at Strike against non-flat-footed creature, deal 1d6 precision damage if using weapon/unarmed that could deal sneak attack.

### 10th Level Feats
- **Precise Debilitations (Prereq: thief racket, Debilitating Strike):** Add debilitations: target takes additional 2d6 precision damage; target becomes flat-footed.
- **Tactical Debilitations (Prereq: scoundrel racket, Debilitating Strike):** Add debilitations: target can't use reactions; target can't flank or contribute to flanking.
- **Vicious Debilitations (Prereq: ruffian racket, Debilitating Strike):** Add debilitations: target gains weakness 5 to bludgeoning/piercing/slashing; target becomes clumsy 1.

### 12th Level Feats
- **Critical Debilitation (Prereq: Debilitating Strike):** On crit hit with sneak attack using Debilitating Strike, add: target Fortitude save - slowed 1 (success), slowed 2 (failure), paralyzed (crit failure).
- **Fantastic Leap:** Launch through air at foe. Attempt High/Long Jump; determine distance as Long Jump. At end of jump, make melee Strike. Fall to ground after Strike if in air; take no damage if fall distance <= jump height.
- **Felling Shot:** Strike with ranged/thrown weapon against flat-footed creature. On success dealing damage: Reflex save - falls up to 120 feet (failure), can't fly/jump/levitate until end of next turn (crit failure).
- **Reactive Interference (Reaction):** Trigger adjacent enemy begins using reaction. If enemy level <= yours, disrupt reaction. If higher, attack roll vs AC; on success disrupt.
- **Spring from the Shadows (Flourish):** Stride up to Speed ending next to hidden/undetected enemy, then Strike that enemy. Remain hidden/undetected until after Strike. Can use while Burrowing/Climbing/Flying/Swimming.

### 14th Level Feats
- **Defensive Roll (Reaction):** Trigger physical attack would reduce you to 0 HP. Take half damage from triggering attack.
- **Instant Opening:** Distract opponent with words/gesture. Choose target within 30 feet; flat-footed against your attacks until end of next turn. Gains auditory or visual trait based on description.
- **Leave an Opening:** On crit hit against flat-footed opponent with melee attack dealing damage, trigger Attack of Opportunity from one ally of your choice who has that reaction.
- **Sense the Unseen (Reaction):** Trigger fail Seek check. Automatically sense any undetected creatures in area where seeking, making them merely hidden.

### 16th Level Feats
- **Blank Slate (Prereq: legendary Deception):** Detection/revelation/scrying effects pass over you unless counteract level 20+. Detect magic detects other magic but not yours; true seeing doesn't reveal you; locate/scrying won't find you.
- **Cloud Step (Prereq: legendary Acrobatics):** When Striding, move across water/air/solid surfaces with limited weight capacity as if normal ground. Sink/fall/break surfaces at end of turn or when using hostile action.
- **Cognitive Loophole (Reaction):** Trigger your turn ends. Requirements specify trigger like Ready action; choose spell 4th level or lower from any tradition during daily preparations. When trigger occurs, spell comes into effect targeting only you.
- **Dispellic Slice:** Strike against flat-footed creature. If sneak attack damage dealt, counteract one spell active on target. Counteract level = rogue level; check modifier = class DC - 10.
- **Perfect Distraction:** Sneak while leaving decoy acting as mislead spell (you're undetected, not invisible). Concentrate to move decoy. Need 10 minutes to set up new decoy after use.

### 18th Level Feats
- **Implausible Infiltration (Prereq: legendary Acrobatics, Quick Squeeze):** Find tiny holes/imperfections to fit through walls/floors/ceilings. Fails if material other than wood/plaster/stone; thicker than 10 feet; or contains metal layer. Can use climb Speed for ceilings.
- **Magical Move (Reaction):** Trigger specify during daily preparations. Requirements specify trigger like Ready action; choose spell 4th level or lower with cost <= 10 minutes casting time, targeting single creature valid for you. When trigger occurs, spell affects only you. Expended after use until next daily preparations.
- **Powerful Sneak:** On Strike using Strength modifier dealing sneak attack damage, change sneak attack from precision to ordinary damage of same type as Strike.
- **Trickster's Ace (Reaction):** Trigger specify during daily preparations. When hit by attack or targeted by effect within area, use Perfect Distraction even if observed, as long as end Sneak movement concealed or in cover. Decoy targeted instead of you; both targeted for area effects if not moved out.

### 20th Level Feats
- **Hidden Paragon (Reaction) (Prereq: legendary Stealth):** Trigger successfully Hide from all foes or Sneak undetected to all foes. Become invisible for 1 minute even using hostile action. Not revealed by glitterdust/see invisibility; creatures can still Seek to locate.
- **Impossible Striker (Prereq: Sly Striker):** Nothing prevents sneak attack even if opponent sees every blow. Deal full sneak attack damage instead of Sly Striker's 1d6, even against non-flat-footed targets.
- **Reactive Distraction (Reaction) (Prereq: legendary Deception, Perfect Distraction):** Trigger would be hit by attack or targeted by effect; within area. Requirements have Perfect Distraction ready. Use Perfect Distraction even if observed; decoy targeted instead of you. Both targeted for area effects if not moved out.

## Skill Feats

Rogues gain access to all skill feats, plus several rogue-specific skill feats tied to their expertise in Stealth, Thievery, Deception, and other skills. Key skill feats include:

- **Acrobatic Tumble:** Move through threatened squares without triggering reactions
- **Fast Stealth:** Use Stealth to Sneak at full Speed
- **Opportune Riposte:** Make attack of opportunity when enemy misses you
- **Slippery Mind:** Second chance on Will saves against mental effects
