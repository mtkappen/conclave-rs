# Stealth Skill Feats

## Level 1 Feats

### Experienced Smuggler FEAT 1
**Prerequisites:** trained in Stealth  
Conceal items from observers more effectively; take a -2 penalty to others' Perception checks to find concealed items.

### Terrain Stalker FEAT 1
**Prerequisites:** trained in Stealth  
Sneak in certain terrain without attempting a check (GM's choice of terrain type).

## Level 2 Feats

### Quiet Allies FEAT 2
**Prerequisites:** Expert in Stealth  
Roll a single Stealth check when sneaking with allies; all allies use your result.

## Level 7 Feats

### Foil Senses FEAT 7
**Prerequisites:** Master in Stealth  
Take precautions against special senses; creatures with unusual senses have difficulty detecting you.

### Swift Sneak FEAT 7
**Prerequisites:** Master in Stealth  
Move your full Speed while you Sneak instead of half Speed.

## Level 15 Feats

### Legendary Sneak FEAT 15
**Prerequisites:** legendary in Stealth, Swift Sneak  
Hide and Sneak without cover or being concealed; you can become hidden even in plain sight with proper preparation.
