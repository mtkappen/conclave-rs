# Performance (Charisma)

Performance measures your ability to entertain others through artistic expression, including music, dance, theater, and other forms of performance.

## Untrained Actions

### Perform [one-action]
You attempt to entertain an audience with a performance. This could be playing an instrument, singing, dancing, telling stories, or any other form of artistic expression. The GM sets the DC based on the sophistication of the audience and the quality of your performance.

- **Critical Success:** Your performance captivates the audience; they are thoroughly entertained and might offer generous rewards or applause.
- **Success:** Your performance entertains the audience adequately; they appreciate your efforts.
- **Failure:** Your performance falls flat; the audience is unimpressed.
- **Critical Failure:** Your performance is so poor that the audience boos you or asks you to leave.

**Performance DC Examples:**
| Audience | Typical DC |
|----------|------------|
| Commoners on the street | 10-15 (Level 0 task) |
| Artisans with refined tastes | 15-20 (Level 2-3 task) |
| Merchants and minor nobility | 20-25 (Level 4-6 task) |
| Royalty and high nobility | 25+ (Level 7+ task) |

## Trained Actions

### Earn Income - DOWNTIME
You use Performance to earn money by entertaining audiences. This works like the general Earn Income activity, but specifically through performances rather than labor or crafting. The GM determines the task level based on the audiences available and your reputation.

- **Critical Success:** You deliver an outstanding performance that earns premium pay; earn income as if the task were 1 level higher.
- **Success:** You deliver a quality performance that earns standard pay for the task level.
- **Failure:** Your performance is mediocre and earns reduced pay or might not find an audience at all.
- **Critical Failure:** Your performance is so poor you earn nothing and might damage your reputation, making it harder to find future work.

**Sample Performance Tasks:**
| Task | Proficiency |
|------|-------------|
| Busking on street corners | Trained |
| Performing at local taverns or inns | Expert |
| Entertaining at noble courts | Master |
| Performing for royalty or legendary venues | Legendary |
