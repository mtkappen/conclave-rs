# Spell Lists Overview

This section provides an overview of the spell lists available to different spellcasting classes in D&D 5e. Each class has its own unique spell list, though there is some overlap between them.

## Bard Spell List

Bards are versatile spellcasters who draw on artistic expression and performance. Their spells focus on enchantment, illusion, and support magic.

**Notable Spells:**
- **Cantrips:** Dancing Lights, Light, Mage Hand, Mending, Message, Minor Illusion, Prestidigitation, Vicious Mockery
- **1st Level:** Cure Wounds, Disguise Self, Healing Word, Heroism, Sleep, Thunderwave
- **2nd Level:** Enhance Ability, Hold Person, Invisibility, Mirror Image
- **3rd Level:** Dispel Magic, Fear, Hypnotic Pattern, Leomund's Tiny Hut
- **4th Level:** Compulsion, Freedom of Movement, Greater Invisibility, Polymorph
- **5th Level:** Dominate Person, Geas, Mass Cure Wounds, Scrying
- **6th Level:** Eyebite, True Seeing
- **7th Level:** Etherealness, Project Image
- **8th Level:** Dominate Monster, Feeblemind
- **9th Level:** Foresight, Power Word Heal

## Cleric Spell List

Clerics are divine spellcasters who draw power from their deity. Their spells focus on healing, protection, and smiting evil.

**Notable Spells:**
- **Cantrips:** Guidance, Light, Mending, Resistance, Sacred Flame, Spare the Dying, Thaumaturgy
- **1st Level:** Bless, Command, Cure Wounds, Guiding Bolt, Healing Word, Shield of Faith
- **2nd Level:** Aid, Find Traps, Lesser Restoration, Spiritual Weapon
- **3rd Level:** Dispel Magic, Mass Healing Word, Revivify, Spirit Guardians
- **4th Level:** Banishment, Death Ward, Freedom of Movement, Guardian of Faith
- **5th Level:** Flame Strike, Greater Restoration, Mass Cure Wounds, Raise Dead
- **6th Level:** Blade Barrier, Heal, Harm, Heroes' Feast
- **7th Level:** Divine Word, Regenerate, Resurrection
- **8th Level:** Control Weather, Holy Aura
- **9th Level:** Mass Heal, True Resurrection

## Druid Spell List

Druids draw their magic from nature itself. Their spells focus on controlling the elements, communicating with animals, and transforming into beasts.

**Notable Spells:**
- **Cantrips:** Druidcraft, Guidance, Mending, Resistance, Shillelagh, Spare the Dying
- **1st Level:** Cure Wounds, Entangle, Faerie Fire, Goodberry, Longstrider, Thunderwave
- **2nd Level:** Barkskin, Enhance Ability, Flaming Sphere, Lesser Restoration, Moonbeam
- **3rd Level:** Call Lightning, Daylight, Dispel Magic, Plant Growth, Water Breathing
- **4th Level:** Blight, Confusion, Conjure Woodland Beings, Freedom of Movement
- **5th Level:** Commune with Nature, Contagion, Insect Plague, Mass Cure Wounds
- **6th Level:** Heal, Heroes' Feast, Transport via Plants, Wall of Thorns
- **7th Level:** Fire Storm, Plane Shift, Regenerate
- **8th Level:** Control Weather, Earthquake, Sunburst
- **9th Level:** Storm of Vengeance, True Resurrection

## Paladin Spell List

Paladins are divine warriors who combine martial prowess with holy magic. Their spells focus on protection, healing, and smiting evil.

**Notable Spells:**
- **1st Level:** Bless, Command, Cure Wounds, Detect Evil and Good, Divine Favor, Shield of Faith
- **2nd Level:** Aid, Find Steed, Lesser Restoration, Zone of Truth
- **3rd Level:** Aura of Vitality, Dispel Magic, Mass Healing Word, Revivify
- **4th Level:** Aura of Life, Death Ward, Freedom of Movement
- **5th Level:** Commune, Flame Strike, Geas, Greater Restoration

## Ranger Spell List

Rangers are wilderness warriors who draw on nature magic. Their spells focus on survival, tracking, and ranged combat enhancement.

**Notable Spells:**
- **1st Level:** Animal Friendship, Cure Wounds, Entangle, Hunter's Mark, Longstrider
- **2nd Level:** Enhance Ability, Find Traps, Lesser Restoration, Pass without Trace
- **3rd Level:** Conjure Animals, Dispel Magic, Plant Growth, Wind Wall
- **4th Level:** Freedom of Movement, Locate Creature, Stone Shape
- **5th Level:** Commune with Nature, Mass Cure Wounds, Tree Stride

## Sorcerer Spell List

Sorcerers are innate spellcasters whose magic comes from within. Their spells focus on raw magical power and manipulation.

**Notable Spells:**
- **Cantrips:** Acid Splash, Fire Bolt, Light, Mage Hand, Ray of Frost, Shocking Grasp
- **1st Level:** Burning Hands, Charm Person, Color Spray, Detect Magic, Disguise Self, Magic Missile, Shield, Sleep, Thunderwave
- **2nd Level:** Blur, Darkvision, Detect Thoughts, Scorching Ray, Shatter
- **3rd Level:** Blink, Fireball, Fly, Haste, Lightning Bolt, Slow
- **4th Level:** Blight, Confusion, Dimension Door, Polymorph
- **5th Level:** Cone of Cold, Dominate Person, Telekinesis
- **6th Level:** Disintegrate, Eyebite, Globe of Invulnerability
- **7th Level:** Finger of Death, Plane Shift
- **8th Level:** Power Word Stun, Sunburst
- **9th Level:** Foresight, Power Word Kill, Time Stop

## Warlock Spell List

Warlocks draw their magic from otherworldly patrons. Their spells focus on deception, control, and eldritch power.

**Notable Spells:**
- **Cantrips:** Chill Touch, Eldritch Blast, Mage Hand, Minor Illusion, Poison Spray, Prestidigitation
- **1st Level:** Charm Person, Expeditious Retreat, Hellish Rebuke, Hex, Ray of Enfeeblement
- **2nd Level:** Blindness/Deafness, Darkness, Detect Thoughts, Hold Person
- **3rd Level:** Counterspell, Dispel Magic, Fly, Hunger of Hadar, Lightning Bolt
- **4th Level:** Banishment, Blight, Dimension Door, Fear
- **5th Level:** Contact Other Plane, Dominate Person, Scrying
