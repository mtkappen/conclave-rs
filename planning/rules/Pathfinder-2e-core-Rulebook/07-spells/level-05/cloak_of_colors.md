# CLOAK OF COLORS

## Spell 5 - Illusion Visual

**Traditions:** arcane, occult  
**Cast:** [two-actions] somatic, verbal  
**Range:** 30 feet; **Targets:** 1 creature  
**Duration:** 1 minute  

A cloak of swirling colors shrouds the target. Creatures are dazzled while adjacent to it, and attacking the target causes the attacker to become confused for 1 round unless they succeed at a Will save.
