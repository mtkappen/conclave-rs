# Smith-Shimano Corpro (SSC)

SSC mechs are fast, agile, flexible, and often able to turn invisible.

Smith-Shimano Corpro is a leading manufacturer of advanced, agile mech designs specializing in mobility, stealth, and electronic warfare capabilities. Their frames are favored by pilots who value speed and adaptability over raw durability.

## SSC Core Bonuses
SSC bonuses enhance your mech's mobility, stealth capabilities, and electronic warfare effectiveness. You must have at least three license levels in SSC licenses to take a bonus from this list.

### Cloaking System
Your mech gains the ability to turn INVISIBLE as a protocol. While invisible, you gain +2� on all attacks made before revealing yourself. This effect ends if you make an attack or force a save.

### Enhanced Sensors
Your mech's SENSORS increase by 5. Additionally, you can detect HIDDEN characters within your SENSORS range without needing to SEARCH.

### Overclocked Systems
Once per mission, you may OVERCHARGE without taking heat. Additionally, your HEAT CAP increases by 2.
