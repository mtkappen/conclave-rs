# Deception Skill Feats

## Level 1 Feats
- **Charming Liar FEAT 1** - Improve a target's attitude with your lies
- **Lengthy Diversion FEAT 1** - Remain hidden after you Create a Diversion
- **Lie to Me FEAT 1** - Use Deception to detect lies

## Level 2 Feats
- **Confabulator FEAT 2** (Expert) - Reduce the bonuses against your repeated lies
- **Quick Disguise FEAT 2** (Expert) - Set up a disguise in only half the time

## Level 7 Feats
- **Slippery Secrets FEAT 7** (Master) - Evade attempts to uncover your true nature
