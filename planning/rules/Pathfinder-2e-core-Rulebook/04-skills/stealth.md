# Stealth (Dexterity)

Stealth measures your ability to avoid detection and move silently.

## Actions

### Conceal an Object [one-action]
You attempt to hide a small object on your person from casual observation. This is typically used when you want to keep something hidden from someone who might search you or see you carrying it.

- **Success:** The object is concealed from casual observation. Someone would need to actively search for it or succeed at a Perception check against your Stealth DC to notice it.
- **Failure:** The object is visible or easily discovered.

### Hide [one-action] - CONCEALED
**Requirements:** You are concealed from the creature you're hiding from, such as by being hidden behind cover or in darkness.

You attempt to become hidden from a particular observer. Make a Stealth check against the observer's Perception DC.

- **Critical Success:** You become undetected by the observer.
- **Success:** You become hidden from the observer.
- **Failure:** You remain observed.
- **Critical Failure:** The observer notices you and any objects you're concealing.

### Sneak [one-action] - MOVE
**Requirements:** You are hidden or undetected by at least one creature in the area.

You Stride up to your Speed while attempting to remain hidden or undetected. Make a Stealth check against the Perception DC of each creature that you're trying to remain hidden from or undetected by. You must be concealed from each such creature (such as by being behind cover, in darkness, or otherwise obscured).

- **Critical Success:** You move up to your Speed and remain undetected by all creatures you attempted to sneak from.
- **Success:** You move up to your Speed and remain hidden from all creatures you attempted to sneak from.
- **Failure:** You move up to your Speed but become observed by some or all of the creatures you were trying to avoid.
- **Critical Failure:** As failure, and you reveal your position to all creatures in the area.

## Stealth DC Examples

| Situation | Typical DC |
|-----------|------------|
| Hiding behind light cover in plain sight | 15-20 |
| Sneaking past guards on patrol | 20-25 |
| Moving undetected through a guarded facility | 25-30 |
| Infiltrating a heavily secured location | 30+ |

## Notes

- You can't Hide while observed. You must first become concealed (by ducking behind cover, stepping into darkness, etc.), then use the Hide action to become hidden or undetected.
- Once hidden or undetected, you can use Sneak to move while maintaining that state.
- Making a hostile action (like attacking) usually reveals your position and ends your hidden/undetected state.
