# Circle of Death

**Level:** 6  
**Traditions:** Necromancy, Dark  
**Cast:** [three-actions] material, somatic, verbal  
**Range:** 120 feet; Area: 40-foot burst  
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude  

You unleash a wave of negative energy that snuffs out life. Each creature in the area must attempt a Fortitude save.

- **Critical Success** The creature is unaffected.
- **Success** The creature takes 12d6 negative damage.
- **Failure** The creature takes 20d6 negative damage and is drained 1.
- **Critical Failure** The creature takes 24d6 negative damage, is drained 2, and if this reduces it to 0 Hit Points, it dies instantly.

**Heightened (+1)** The initial damage increases by 2d6, and the additional damage on a failure or critical failure increases by 2d6.
