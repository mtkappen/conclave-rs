# Clone

**Level:** 9  
**Traditions:** Necromancy, Transmutation  
**Cast:** 1 hour (material, somatic, verbal); Cost: 5,000 gp worth of rare materials and a flask of special nutrient solution  
**Range:** touch; Targets: 1 living creature and a sample of its flesh  
**Duration:** until the clone matures  

You create a genetic duplicate of the target creature in a flask of nutrient solution. The clone grows to full maturity in 2-3 months. When the original creature dies, its soul transfers to the clone if it is within range and willing (or unwilling, depending on alignment).

The clone has all the memories and abilities of the original at the time of death, but none of the equipment or magical effects. The clone is a separate creature with its own identity, though it shares the original's memories up to the point of cloning.

**Heightened (+1)** The cost increases by 2,000 gp.
