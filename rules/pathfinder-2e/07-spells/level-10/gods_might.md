# Gods Might

**Level:** 10  
**Traditions:** Divine, Transmutation  
**Cast:** [two-actions] somatic, verbal; Requirements: You have a deity  
**Duration:** 1 minute  

You channel the combined power of your deity and other divine beings, becoming an avatar of divine might. You gain the following benefits:

- AC = 32 + your level. Ignore your armor's check penalty and Speed reduction.
- 60 temporary Hit Points.

- All your attacks deal an additional 3d12 positive damage.
- You gain a fly Speed of 150 feet.

- You gain resistance 25 to all damage types except force and ghost touch.
- Your unarmed attacks become divine weapons with the following statistics: Melee [one-action] divine strike, Attack +40, Damage 8d12+20 positive.

You can cast any divine spell you know as if it were heightened to 10th level. Additionally, once per round, you can use a free action to cast two 9th-level or lower divine spells without expending spell slots.
**Heightened (+1)** The temporary Hit Points increase by 15 and the resistance increases by 5.
