# Symbol

**Level:** 8  
**Traditions:** Arcane, Divine, Occult  
**Cast:** 10 minutes (material, somatic, verbal)  
**Range:** touch; Targets: 1 surface or object up to 10 square feet  
**Duration:** until triggered or dispelled  

You inscribe a powerful magical symbol that triggers when certain conditions are met. When casting, choose one of the following effects and set a trigger condition (such as "when a creature touches this" or "when a creature of evil alignment reads this"):

- **Death:** Kills creatures that trigger it (Fortitude negates)
- **Discordance:** Causes confusion and distraction (Will negates)
- **Fear:** Frightens creatures (Will negates)
- **Hopelessness:** Makes creatures unable to take hostile actions (Will negates)
- **Pain:** Inflicts crippling pain (Fortitude negates)
- **Sleep:** Puts creatures to sleep (Will negates)
- **Stunning:** Stuns creatures (Will negates)

The symbol can be made visible or invisible. It can be triggered by a password you set, which prevents unintended activations. A symbol lasts until triggered or dispelled, up to 10 years.

**Heightened (+1)** The save DC increases by 1.
