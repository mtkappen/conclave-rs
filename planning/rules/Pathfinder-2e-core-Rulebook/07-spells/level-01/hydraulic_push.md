# HYDRAULIC PUSH

## Spell 1 - Attack Evocation Water

**Traditions:** arcane, primal  
**Cast:** [two-actions] somatic, verbal  
**Range:** 60 feet; **Targets:** 1 creature or object  

You blast the target with a jet of water. Make a spell attack roll against the target's AC. On a hit, you deal 2d8 bludgeoning damage and push the target up to 15 feet away from you. If the target is a creature, it must attempt a Fortitude save.

- **Critical Success** The target is unaffected.
- **Success** The target takes half damage and isn't pushed.
- **Failure** The target takes full damage and is pushed 5 feet.
- **Critical Failure** The target takes double damage and is pushed 15 feet.

**Heightened (+1):** The damage increases by 2d8, and the push distance increases by 5 feet.
