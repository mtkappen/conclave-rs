# Solid Fog

**Conjuration Water**
**Traditions:** arcane, primal
**Cast:** [three-actions] material, somatic, verbal
**Range:** 120 feet; **Area:** 20-foot burst
**Duration:** 1 minute

You conjure a bank of fog so thick it impedes movement as well as sight. This functions as obscuring mist (page 355), except that the area is also difficult terrain. You can Dismiss the spell.
