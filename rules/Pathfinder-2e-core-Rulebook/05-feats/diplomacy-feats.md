# Diplomacy Skill Feats

## Level 1 Feats
- **Bargain Hunter FEAT 1** - Earn Income by searching for deals
- **Group Impression FEAT 1** - Make an Impression on multiple targets at once
- **Hobnobber FEAT 1** - Gather Information rapidly

## Level 2 Feats
- **Glad-Hand FEAT 2** (Expert) - Make an Impression on a target you've just met

## Level 7 Feats
- **Shameless Request FEAT 7** (Master) - Make Requests of others with lesser consequences

## Level 15 Feats
- **Legendary Negotiation FEAT 15** (Legendary) - Quickly parley with foes
