# Survival Skill Feats

## Level 1 Feats
- **Experienced Tracker FEAT 1** - Track at your full Speed at a –5 penalty
- **Forager FEAT 1** - Forage for supplies to provide for multiple creatures
- **Survey Wildlife FEAT 1** - Identify nearby creatures through signs and clues
- **Terrain Expertise FEAT 1** - +1 to Survival checks in certain terrain

## Level 7 Feats
- **Planar Survival FEAT 7** (Master) - Use Survival to Subsist on different planes

## Level 15 Feats
- **Legendary Survivalist FEAT 15** (Legendary) - Survive extreme conditions
