# Invisibility Sphere

## Spell 3 - Illusion
**Traditions:** arcane, occult  
**Cast:** [two-actions] material, somatic, verbal  
**Area:** 10-foot emanation; **Targets:** you and any number of creatures in range  
**Duration:** 10 minutes  

Cloaked in illusion, the targets become invisible. This makes them undetected to all creatures, though the creatures can attempt to find the targets, making them hidden to them instead. If a target uses a hostile action, the spell ends after that hostile action is completed.
**Heightened (4th):** The spell lasts 1 minute, but it doesn't end if the target uses a hostile action.
