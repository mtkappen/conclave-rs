# Telekinesis

**Level:** 5  
**Traditions:** Arcane, Occult  
**Cast:** [two-actions] somatic, verbal  
**Range:** varies; Targets: 1 creature or object  

You manipulate objects and creatures with your mind. You can use this spell in one of three ways:
- **Sustained Move** You move an unattended object of up to 80 Bulk up to 20 feet per round. The object must be within 120 feet of you.

- **Attack** You make a ranged spell attack against a creature or object. On a hit, you deal 4d6 force damage and can move the target up to 20 feet in any direction.
- **Sustained Hold** You hold a creature or object in place. The target must attempt a Fortitude save. On a failure, it is immobilized for as long as you sustain the spell.
**Heightened (+1)** The damage increases by 1d6, and the Bulk limit increases by 20.
