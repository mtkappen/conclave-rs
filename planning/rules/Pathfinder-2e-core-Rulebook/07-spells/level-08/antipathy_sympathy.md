# Antipathy/Sympathy

**Level:** 8  
**Traditions:** Arcane, Occult  
**Cast:** 10 minutes (material, somatic, verbal)  
**Range:** touch; Targets: 1 object or area up to 20-foot cube  
**Duration:** 10 days  

You cause a particular type of creature to feel either repelled or attracted to the target. Choose antipathy or sympathy when you cast the spell, and specify the type of creature affected (such as "dragons" or "humanoids").

- **Antipathy:** Affected creatures that come within 100 feet of the target must attempt a Will save. On a failure, they are frightened 2 and must use their actions to move away from the target as quickly as possible.
- **Synergy:** Affected creatures that come within 100 feet of the target must attempt a Will save. On a failure, they are fascinated by the target and drawn toward it.

Affected creatures can attempt a new save at the end of each of their turns. Creatures that succeed at two consecutive saves are temporarily immune for 24 hours.

**Heightened (+1)** The duration increases by 5 days.
