## Quick Reference

### Dice Mechanics
- **Skill Check:** 1d20 + modifiers vs 10 target number
- **Attack Roll:** 1d20 + GRIT/TECH ATTACK vs defense (EVASION/E-DEFENSE)
- **Save:** 1d20 + GRIT vs attacker's save target
- **Accuracy/Difficulty:** Add/remove d6, keep highest

### Key Statistics
- **GRIT:** Half LL rounded up
- **HP:** Pilot: 6 + GRIT; Mech: varies by frame
- **STRUCTURE:** Most PC mechs have 4
- **STRESS:** Most PC mechs have 4
- **REPAIR CAP:** Frame-based + HULL bonus

### Action Economy (Mech Combat)
- **Standard Move:** Up to SPEED spaces
- **Quick Actions:** 2 per turn
- **Full Actions:** 1 per turn (replaces 2 quick actions)
- **Reactions:** 1 per turn, multiple per round
- **Free Actions:** Unlimited on your turn
