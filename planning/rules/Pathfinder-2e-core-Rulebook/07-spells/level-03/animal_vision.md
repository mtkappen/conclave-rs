# ANIMAL VISION

## Spell 3 - Divination Mental

**Traditions:** primal  
**Cast:** 1 minute (material, somatic, verbal)  
**Range:** 120 feet; **Targets:** 1 animal  
**Duration:** 1 hour  

You tap into the target's senses, allowing you to see, hear, and otherwise sense whatever it senses for the spell's duration. If the target wishes to prevent you from doing so, it can attempt a Will save; on a success, the spell fails.
