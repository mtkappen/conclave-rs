# CREATE FOOD

## Spell 2 - Conjuration

**Traditions:** arcane, divine, primal  
**Cast:** [two-actions] somatic, verbal  
**Range:** touch; **Area:** 10-foot burst  

You create enough food to feed six Medium creatures for a day. This food is bland and unappealing, but it is nourishing. After 1 day, if no one has eaten the food, it decays and becomes inedible. Most Small creatures eat one-quarter as much, Large creatures eat twice as much, and Huge or larger creatures eat four times as much.
