# Deity Form

**Level:** 10  
**Traditions:** Divine, Transmutation  
**Cast:** [two-actions] somatic, verbal  
**Duration:** 1 minute  

You transform into a deity-like being of immense power. You gain the following benefits:

- AC = 30 + your level. Ignore your armor's check penalty and Speed reduction.
- 50 temporary Hit Points.
- Darkvision and low-light vision.
- All your attacks deal an additional 2d10 damage of a type appropriate to your deity (fire, cold, electricity, positive, negative, etc.).
- You gain a fly Speed of 120 feet.
- You gain resistance 20 to all damage types except force and ghost touch.
- Your unarmed attacks become divine weapons with the following statistics: Melee [one-action] divine strike, Attack +35, Damage 6d12+15 of appropriate type.

You can cast any divine spell you know as if it were heightened to 10th level. Additionally, once per round, you can use a free action to cast a 9th-level or lower divine spell without expending a spell slot.

**Heightened (+1)** The temporary Hit Points increase by 10 and the resistance increases by 5.
