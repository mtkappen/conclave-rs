# Lay On Hands

**Level:** Focus 1 (Champion)  
**Traditions:** Healing, Necromancy, Positive  
**Cast:** [one-action] somatic  
**Range:** touch; Targets: 1 willing living creature or 1 undead creature  

Your hands become infused with positive energy, healing a living creature or damaging an undead creature with a touch. If you use lay on hands on a willing living target, you restore 6 Hit Points; if the target is one of your allies, they also gain a +1 status bonus to AC and saving throws for 1 round.
**Heightened (+1)** The healing increases by 2 Hit Points.
