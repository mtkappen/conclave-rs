# Animal Form

**Spell Level** 2  
**Traditions** Primal  
**Cast** [two-actions] somatic, verbal  
**Duration** 1 minute  

You call upon primal energy to transform yourself into a Medium animal battle form. When you first cast this spell, choose ape, bear, bull, canine, cat, deer, frog, shark, or snake. You can decide the specific type of animal (such as lion or snow leopard for cat), but this has no effect on the form's Size or statistics. While in this form, you gain the animal trait. You can Dismiss the spell.

You gain the following statistics and abilities regardless of which battle form you choose:
- AC = 16 + your level. Ignore your armor's check penalty and Speed reduction.
- 5 temporary Hit Points.
- Low-light vision and imprecise scent 30 feet.
- One or more unarmed melee attacks specific to the battle form you choose, which are the only attacks you can use. You're trained with them. Your attack modifier is +9, and your damage bonus is +1. These attacks are Strength based (for the purpose of the enfeebled condition, for example). If your unarmed attack bonus is higher, you can use it instead.
- Athletics modifier of +9, unless your own modifier is higher.

You also gain specific abilities based on the form you choose:
- **Ape** Speed 25 feet; Melee [one-action] fist (agile), Damage 1d8 bludgeoning plus Grab.
- **Bear** Speed 30 feet; Melee [one-action] bite, Damage 1d8 piercing; Melee [one-action] claw (agile), Damage 1d6 slashing.
- **Bull** Speed 40 feet; Melee [one-action] horn, Damage 1d8 piercing plus Shove.
- **Canine** Speed 40 feet; Melee [one-action] bite, Damage 1d8 piercing plus Trip.
- **Cat** Speed 35 feet; Melee [one-action] claw (agile), Damage 1d6 slashing.
- **Deer** Speed 50 feet; Melee [one-action] antlers, Damage 1d8 piercing.
- **Frog** Speed 25 feet, swim 25 feet; Melee [one-action] bite, Damage 1d6 piercing plus Grab and Pull.
- **Shark** Swim 60 feet; Melee [one-action] bite, Damage 1d10 piercing.
- **Snake** Speed 25 feet, climb 25 feet; Melee [one-action] fangs, Damage 1d6 piercing plus 1d4 persistent poison.

**Heightened (3rd)** Your battle form is Large and your attacks gain a 5-foot reach. You must have enough space to expand into or the spell is lost. You instead gain AC = 19 + your level, 10 temporary HP, attack modifier +12, damage bonus +4, and Athletics +13.

**Heightened (4th)** Your battle form is Huge. You must have enough space to expand into or the spell is lost. You instead gain AC = 21 + your level, 15 temporary HP, attack modifier +16, damage bonus +8, and Athletics +17.
