# Duplicate Foe

## Spell 7 - Conjuration
**Traditions:** arcane, occult  
**Cast:** [three-actions] material, somatic, verbal  
**Range:** 30 feet; **Targets:** 1 enemy of level 15 or lower  
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude; **Duration:** sustained up to 1 minute  

You create a duplicate of the target that fights for you. The duplicate has the same statistics as the target, but it is a construct with the summoned trait. It obeys your commands and attacks your enemies.
