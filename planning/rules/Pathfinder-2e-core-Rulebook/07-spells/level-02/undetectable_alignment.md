# Undetectable Alignment

**Spell Level** 2  
**Rarity** Uncommon  
**Traditions** Divine, Occult  
**Cast** 1 minute (material, somatic, verbal)  
**Range** Touch; **Targets** 1 creature or object  

You mask the target's alignment aura from detection. The target's alignment is hidden from all effects that would reveal it, including detect alignment spells and similar abilities.
