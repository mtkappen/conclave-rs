# HORUS

HORUS mechs are esoteric constructs, specialized to control the battlefield – hacking, inflicting statuses, and dealing heat.

HORUS is a mysterious manufacturer known for producing some of the most unconventional and technologically advanced mechs in the galaxy. Their designs often incorporate experimental technologies, non-human person integration, and reality-bending capabilities that defy conventional engineering principles. HORUS pilots are typically those who seek to push the boundaries of what's possible in mech combat, embracing the unknown and the esoteric.

## HORUS Core Bonuses
HORUS bonuses focus on electronic warfare, heat manipulation, and battlefield control through unconventional means. You must have at least three license levels in HORUS licenses to take a bonus from this list.

### Cascade Resistant
Your mech gains IMMUNITY to cascades. Additionally, NHPs installed in your mech cannot enter cascade unless you choose to let them go.

### Heat Sink Enhancement
Your HEAT CAP increases by 2. Additionally, once per mission when you overheat, you may choose to clear all heat instead of making an overheating check.
