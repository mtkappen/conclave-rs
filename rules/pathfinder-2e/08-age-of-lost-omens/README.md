## Inner Sea Regions

The Inner Sea region is divided into 13 main regions:

1. **01-absalom.md** - Absalom and Starstone Isle, the City at the Center of the World
2. **02-broken-lands.md** - Region scarred by the Shining Crusade
3. **03-mendev.md** - Crusader kingdom fighting demonic incursions from the Worldwound
4. **04-numeria.md** - Nation shaped by alien technology from a crashed starship
5. **05-razmiran.md** - Theocracy ruled by the Living God Razmir
6. **06-taldor.md** - Former great empire now in decline
7. **07-qadira.md** - Major trading nation and economic powerhouse
8. **08-andoran.md** - Democratic nation championing freedom and liberty
9. **09-cheliax.md** - Infernal empire allied with Hell, ruled by House Thrune
10. **10-molthune.md** - Militaristic nation with aggressive expansionist policies
11. **11-osirion.md** - One of the oldest nations on Golarion, center of the Golden Road
12. **12-katapesh.md** - Trade-focused nation with unique governance and black markets
13. **13-nidal.md** - Shadowy nation devoted to Zon-Kuthon, god of darkness

## Key Historical Events

- **-5293 AR** - Earthfall nearly ended the world
- **1 AR** - Aroden raises Starstone Isle, beginning Absalom Reckoning calendar
- **4606 AR** - Aroden dies, prophecies fail, Age of Lost Omens begins
- **4719 AR** - Current year (corresponds to 2019 CE)

## Calendar

The Inner Sea region uses the Absalom Reckoning calendar with 12 months and 7-day weeks. An extra leap day is added every 4 years.

### Months
Abadius, Calistril, Pharast, Gozran, Desnus, Sarenith, Erastus, Arodus, Rova, Lamashan, Neth, Kuthona

### Days
Moonday, Toilday, Wealday, Oathday, Fireday, Starday, Sunday

## Cosmology

Golarion exists within the Great Beyond, a multiverse of layered planes:

- **Inner Sphere** - Material Plane and Elemental Planes
- **Outer Sphere** - Realm of gods and afterlife
- **Other Dimensions** - Ethereal Plane, Astral Plane, First World, Shadow Plane, Dreamlands, Dimension of Time
