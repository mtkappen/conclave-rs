# Heroes Feast

**Level:** 7  
**Traditions:** Divine, Primal  
**Cast:** 10 minutes (material, somatic, verbal); Cost: 500 gp worth of rare spices and delicacies  
**Range:** touch; Area: 10-foot burst centered on a table or similar surface  
**Duration:** until the next time you make your daily preparations  

You conjure a magnificent feast that fills the area with delicious food and drink. Up to 12 willing creatures can partake of the feast. Each creature that eats the food gains the following benefits for 24 hours:

- The creature is immune to poison and disease.
- The creature doesn't need to eat or drink.

- The creature gains a +1 status bonus to all saving throws.
- The creature is healed 5d8 Hit Points when it finishes eating.

The food disappears if not consumed within 1 hour of being conjured.
**Heightened (+1)** The number of creatures that can benefit increases by 2, and the healing increases by 1d8.
