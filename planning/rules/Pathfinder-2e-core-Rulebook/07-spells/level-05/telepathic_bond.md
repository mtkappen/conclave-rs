# Telepathic Bond

**Level:** 5  
**Traditions:** Arcane, Occult  
**Cast:** 1 minute (material, somatic, verbal)  
**Range:** touch; Targets: you and up to 4 willing creatures touched  
**Duration:** 8 hours  

The targets can communicate telepathically with any or all of the other targets from any point on the same planet. The telepathic connection allows for complex communication including emotions and images, not just words.

If any target moves to a different plane, the bond is broken for that target until they return to the same plane as the others.

**Heightened (+1)** You can target one additional creature.
