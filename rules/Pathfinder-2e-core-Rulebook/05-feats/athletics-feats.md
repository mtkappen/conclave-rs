# Athletics Skill Feats

## Level 1 Feats

### Combat Climber FEAT 1
**Prerequisites:** trained in Athletics  
Your techniques allow you to fight as you climb. You're not flat-footed while Climbing and can Climb with a hand occupied. You must still use another hand and both legs to Climb.

### Hefty Hauler FEAT 1
**Prerequisites:** trained in Athletics  
Increase your Bulk limits by 2.

### Quick Jump FEAT 1
**Prerequisites:** trained in Athletics  
High Jump or Long Jump as a single action instead of two actions.

### Titan Wrestler FEAT 1
**Prerequisites:** trained in Athletics  
Disarm, Grapple, Shove, or Trip larger creatures (up to Huge size).

### Underwater Marauder FEAT 1
**Prerequisites:** trained in Athletics  
Fight more effectively underwater; you're not flat-footed while Swimming.

## Level 2 Feats

### Powerful Leap FEAT 2
**Prerequisites:** Expert in Athletics  
Jump farther and higher; add your level to High Jump and Long Jump distances.

### Rapid Mantel FEAT 2
**Prerequisites:** Expert in Athletics  
Pull yourself onto ledges quickly; Climb as a single action when mantling.

## Level 7 Feats

### Quick Climb FEAT 7
**Prerequisites:** Master in Athletics  
Climb swiftly; you can Climb at your full Speed instead of half Speed.

### Quick Swim FEAT 7
**Prerequisites:** Master in Athletics  
Swim quickly; you can Swim at your full Speed without penalty.

### Wall Jump FEAT 7
**Prerequisites:** Master in Athletics  
Jump off walls to change direction or gain height during a climb.

## Level 15 Feats

### Cloud Jump FEAT 15
**Prerequisites:** legendary in Athletics  
Triple the distance you Long Jump; add your Speed to jump distance for each extra action used.
