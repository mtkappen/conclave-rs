# Permanent Image

**Level:** 6  
**Traditions:** Illusion, Visual  
**Cast:** [three-actions] material, somatic, verbal  
**Range:** 120 feet; Area: 20-foot burst  
**Duration:** unlimited  

You create a static visual illusion of any scene you can imagine. The image includes sight and sound, but no other senses. You can specify a simple program for the image to follow (such as "a guard who paces back and forth"), which repeats continuously.

Any creature that touches the image or uses the Seek action to examine it can attempt to disbelieve your illusion. On a critical success, the creature sees through the entire illusion.

**Heightened (+2)** The area increases by 10 feet per side.
