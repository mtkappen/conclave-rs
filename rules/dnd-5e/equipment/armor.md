# Armor

Armor protects you from harm in combat. Different types of armor offer varying levels of protection, but they also have drawbacks such as reduced mobility or disadvantage on certain checks.

## Armor Categories

### Light Armor
Light armor relies heavily on agility and doesn't hinder movement. It allows you to add your full Dexterity modifier to your AC.

- **Padded:** AC 11 + Dex modifier (Disadvantage on Stealth)
- **Leather:** AC 11 + Dex modifier
- **Studded Leather:** AC 12 + Dex modifier

### Medium Armor
Medium armor offers moderate protection but limits the Dexterity bonus you can add to your AC. You can add up to +2 from your Dexterity modifier to your AC while wearing medium armor.

- **Hide:** AC 12 + Dex modifier (max +2)
- **Chain Shirt:** AC 13 + Dex modifier (max +2)
- **Scale Mail:** AC 14 + Dex modifier (max +2, Disadvantage on Stealth)
- **Breastplate:** AC 14 + Dex modifier (max +2)
- **Half Plate:** AC 15 + Dex modifier (max +2, Disadvantage on Stealth)

### Heavy Armor
Heavy armor provides the best protection but doesn't allow you to add your Dexterity modifier to your AC. Some heavy armor imposes disadvantage on Stealth checks.

- **Ring Mail:** AC 14 (Disadvantage on Stealth)
- **Chain Mail:** AC 16 (Disadvantage on Stealth)
- **Splint:** AC 17 (Disadvantage on Stealth)
- **Plate:** AC 18 (Disadvantage on Stealth)

## Shields

Shields are held in one hand and provide additional protection. Wielding a shield increases your AC by +2. You can benefit from only one shield at a time.

**Shield:** AC +2

## Armor Proficiency

Characters gain proficiency with certain types of armor through their class:
- **Light Armor:** Bards, Clerics, Druids, Rangers, Rogues
- **Medium Armor:** Barbarians, Bards, Clerics, Druids, Monks (no), Paladins, Rangers, Rogues
- **Heavy Armor:** Clerics, Paladins
- **Shields:** Most classes except Monks and Wizards

If you wear armor that you lack proficiency with, you have Disadvantage on any ability check, saving throw, or attack roll that involves Strength or Dexterity, and you can't cast spells.

## Armor and Stealth

Some armor has the property of imposing Disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks. This represents noisy or cumbersome armor that makes it difficult to move silently.

## Unarmored Defense

Some classes have features that allow them to calculate AC without wearing armor:
- **Barbarian:** 10 + Dex modifier + Con modifier
- **Monk:** 10 + Dex modifier + Wis modifier
- **Draconic Sorcerer:** 13 + Dex modifier (with dragon scales)

These features can't be combined with armor or shields unless specifically stated.
