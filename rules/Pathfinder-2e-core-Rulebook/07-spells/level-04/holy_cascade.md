# Holy Cascade

## Spell 4 - Evocation Good Positive
**Traditions:** divine  
**Cast:** [two-actions] somatic, verbal; **Cost:** one vial of holy water (page 571)  
**Range:** 500 feet; **Area:** 20-foot burst  

A cascade of holy water rains down on the area. Each creature in the area takes 6d6 positive damage and 4d6 good damage. Creatures that are evil or undead take double damage. A basic Reflex save reduces the damage.
**Heightened (+1):** The damage increases by 2d6 (both positive and good).
