# General Skill Feats

These are general feats with the skill trait. They apply to multiple skills or specific skills.

## Level 1 Feats

### Assurance FEAT 1 - FORTUNE GENERAL SKILL
**Prerequisites:** trained in at least one skill  
Even in the worst circumstances, you can perform basic tasks. Choose a skill you're trained in. You can forgo rolling a skill check for that skill to instead receive a result of 10 + your proficiency bonus (do not apply any other bonuses, penalties, or modifiers).

### Bargain Hunter FEAT 1 - GENERAL SKILL
**Prerequisites:** trained in Diplomacy  
You can Earn Income using Diplomacy, spending your days hunting for bargains and reselling at a profit. You can also spend time specifically sniffing out a great bargain on an item; this works as if you were using Earn Income with Diplomacy, except instead of gaining money, you purchase the item at a discount equal to the money you would have gained. Finally, if you select Bargain Hunter during character creation at 1st level, you start play with an additional 2 gp.

### Battle Cry FEAT 7 - GENERAL SKILL
**Prerequisites:** master in Intimidation  
When you roll initiative, you can yell a mighty battle cry and Demoralize an observed foe as a free action. If you're legendary in Intimidation, you can use a reaction to Demoralize your foe when you critically succeed at an attack roll.

### Battle Medicine [one-action] FEAT 1 - GENERAL HEALING MANIPULATE SKILL
**Prerequisites:** trained in Medicine  
You can patch up yourself or an adjacent ally, even in combat. Attempt a Medicine check with the same DC as for Treat Wounds and provide the corresponding amount of healing. As with Treat Wounds, you can attempt checks against higher DCs if you have the minimum proficiency rank. The target is then temporarily immune to your Battle Medicine for 1 day.

### Cat Fall FEAT 1 - GENERAL SKILL
**Prerequisites:** trained in Acrobatics  
Your catlike aerial acrobatics allow you to cushion your falls. Treat falls as 10 feet shorter. If you're an expert in Acrobatics, treat falls as 25 feet shorter. If you're a master in Acrobatics, treat them as 50 feet shorter. If you're legendary in Acrobatics, you always land on your feet and don't take damage, regardless of the distance of the fall.

### Charming Liar FEAT 1 - GENERAL SKILL
**Prerequisites:** trained in Deception  
Your charm allows you to win over those you lie to. When you get a critical success using the Lie action, the target's attitude toward you improves by one step, as though you'd succeeded at using Diplomacy to Make an Impression. This works only once per conversation.

### Cloud Jump FEAT 15 - GENERAL SKILL
**Prerequisites:** legendary in Athletics  
Your unparalleled athletic skill allows you to jump impossible distances. Triple the distance you Long Jump (so you could jump 60 feet on a successful DC 20 check). When you High Jump, use the calculation for a Long Jump but don't triple the distance.

### Combat Climber FEAT 1 - GENERAL SKILL
**Prerequisites:** trained in Athletics  
Your techniques allow you to fight as you climb. You're not flat-footed while Climbing and can Climb with a hand occupied. You must still use another hand and both legs to Climb.

### Confabulator FEAT 2 - GENERAL SKILL
**Prerequisites:** expert in Deception  
Even when caught in falsehoods, you pile lie upon lie. Reduce the bonuses against your repeated lies.

### Craft Anything FEAT 15 - GENERAL SKILL
**Prerequisites:** legendary in Crafting  
You can find ways to craft just about anything, despite restrictions. As long as you have the appropriate Crafting skill feat (such as Magical Crafting for magic items) and meet the item's level and proficiency requirement, you ignore just about any other requirement.

### Dubious Knowledge FEAT 1 - GENERAL SKILL
**Prerequisites:** Trained in Recall Knowledge skill  
Learn true and erroneous knowledge on failed check. When you critically fail a Recall Knowledge check with this feat, you learn some correct information along with some incorrect information.

### Quick Identification FEAT 1 - GENERAL SKILL
**Prerequisites:** Trained in Arcana, Nature, Occultism, or Religion  
Identify Magic in 1 minute or less instead of the normal 10 minutes.

### Recognize Spell FEAT 1 - GENERAL SKILL
**Prerequisites:** Trained in Arcana, Nature, Occultism, or Religion  
Identify a spell as a reaction as it's being cast. When a creature Casts a Spell you can see, you can use your reaction to attempt a skill check to identify the spell.

### Skill Training FEAT 1 - GENERAL SKILL
**Prerequisites:** Intelligence 12  
Become trained in a skill of your choice.

### Trick Magic Item FEAT 1 - GENERAL SKILL
**Prerequisites:** Trained in Arcana, Nature, Occultism, or Religion  
Activate a magic item you normally can't activate. You can use magic items even if you don't meet the requirements, though at a penalty.

## Higher Level Feats

### Automatic Knowledge FEAT 2 - GENERAL SKILL
**Prerequisites:** expert in a skill with the Recall Knowledge action, Assurance in that skill  
You know basic facts off the top of your head. You can use the Recall Knowledge action with that skill as a free action once per round. If you do, you must use Assurance on the skill check.

### Magical Shorthand FEAT 2 - GENERAL SKILL
**Prerequisites:** Expert in Arcana, Nature, Occultism, or Religion  
Learn spells quickly and at a reduced cost. When Learning a Spell, you reduce the material cost by half.

### Quick Recognition FEAT 7 - GENERAL SKILL
**Prerequisites:** Master in Arcana, Nature, Occultism, or Religion; Recognize Spell  
Identify spells as a free action instead of a reaction.
