# Occultism (Intelligence)

Occultism measures your knowledge of ancient mysteries, obscure philosophy, creatures of occult significance, and esoteric planes.

## Untrained Actions

### Recall Knowledge
Recall Knowledge about:
- Ancient mysteries
- Obscure philosophy
- Creatures of occult significance
- Esoteric planes (Astral Plane, Shadow Plane, etc.)

## Trained Actions

### Decipher Writing - EXPLORATION SECRET
When you encounter particularly archaic or esoteric texts, the GM might require you to Decipher the Writing before you can understand it. You must be trained in Occultism to decipher occult texts. This usually takes 1 minute per page of text, but might take longer for complex ciphers or metaphysical writings.

- **Critical Success:** You understand the true meaning of the text, including any hidden or coded messages.
- **Success:** You understand the true meaning of the text. If it was a coded document, you know the general meaning but might not have a word-for-word translation.
- **Failure:** You can't understand the text and take a –2 circumstance penalty to further checks to decipher it.
- **Critical Failure:** You believe you understand the text on that page, but you have in fact misconstrued its message.

### Identify Magic - EXPLORATION SECRET
Using Occultism, you can attempt to identify occult magic, including occult spells, magical items with the occult trait, and creatures with occult abilities. This works like the general Identify Magic action but specifically applies to occult traditions.

- **Critical Success:** You learn all attributes of the occult magic, including its name, effects, and any means of activating it.
- **Success:** For an item or location, you get a sense of what it does and learn any means of activating it. For an ongoing effect, you learn the effect's name and what it does.
- **Failure:** You fail to identify the magic and can't try again for 1 day.
- **Critical Failure:** You misidentify the magic as something else.

### Learn a Spell - EXPLORATION
If you're an occult spellcaster (such as a sorcerer with an occult bloodline or a wizard studying occult magic), you can use Occultism to learn a new spell from the occult tradition. This works like the general Learn a Spell action but uses Occultism instead of Arcana, Religion, or Nature.

To learn the spell:
- Spend 1 hour per level of the spell
- Have materials with the Price indicated in Table 4–3 (Learning a Spell)
- Attempt an Occultism check against the DC determined by the GM
