## Game System Overview

Call of Cthulhu d20 combines:
- The horror atmosphere and Sanity mechanics of Call of Cthulhu
- The d20 System rules from D&D 3rd Edition

### Key Mechanics

| Mechanic | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Ability Scores | STR, DEX, CON, INT, WIS, CHA |
| Skill Checks | 1d20 + Ranks + Ability Modifier |
| Attack Rolls | 1d20 + BAB + Ability Modifier |
| Saving Throws | 1d20 + Base Save + Ability Modifier |
| Sanity | Starts at Wisdom, lost through horror |

### Character Progression

- **Levels**: 1st to 20th
- **Feats**: At 1st, and every level divisible by 3
- **Ability Increases**: Every 4 levels
- **Skills**: 8 + INT modifier per level

## Horror in Call of Cthulhu

Unlike traditional fantasy RPGs:
- Investigators are ordinary humans, not heroes
- Combat is dangerous; running away is often wise
- Knowledge comes at the cost of Sanity
- Victory means survival, not defeating evil
- The Mythos cannot be destroyed, only delayed

## Recommended Play Style

### For Players
- Roleplay your character's fear and uncertainty
- Don't assume you can fight everything
- Value knowledge and information
- Accept that characters may die or go mad

### For GMs
- Build atmosphere through description
- Reward clever play over combat prowess
- Use Sanity loss meaningfully
- Remember: mystery is scarier than revelation
