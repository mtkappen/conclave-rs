# Nidal

North of Cheliax, the shadowy nation of Nidal remains under the church of Zon-Kuthon, the god of darkness. Nidal is the oldest nation on Golarion still ruled by its original founders.

Key features:
- Shadowy nation devoted to Zon-Kuthon
- Oldest continuously inhabited nation
- Ruled by the church of darkness
- Mysterious and secretive culture
