# Osirion

At the center of the Golden Road lies mighty Osirion, one of the oldest nations on Golarion. For a time ruled by Qadira, the legacies and influence of Osirion's artisans, philosophers, and spellcasters have spread far and wide.

Osirion was for a time ruled by Qadira, but has since regained its independence. The nation is known for its ancient pyramids, tombs, and archaeological treasures.

Key features:
- One of the oldest nations on Golarion
- Ancient pyramids and tombs
- Rich archaeological heritage
- Center of the Golden Road trade route
- Skilled artisans and spellcasters
