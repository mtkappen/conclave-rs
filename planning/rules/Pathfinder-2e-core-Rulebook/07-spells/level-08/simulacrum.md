# Simulacrum

**Level:** 8  
**Traditions:** Illusion, Transmutation  
**Cast:** 1 hour (material, somatic, verbal); Cost: 5,000 gp worth of rare gems and powdered ruby  
**Range:** touch; Targets: 1 willing creature  
**Duration:** until dispelled  

You create a partially real illusionary duplicate of the target creature. The simulacrum appears to be the target, but it is clearly made of ice and snow when examined closely. It has half the target's Hit Points (rounded down), uses the same statistics as the target, but can't regain Hit Points except through spells that specifically mention simulacrums.

The simulacrum obeys your commands and acts on your initiative count. It shares your memories and knowledge up to the point of its creation. The simulacrum can cast spells, but if it casts a spell with a cost or that restores resources, those costs must be paid separately and it doesn't regain those resources.

When the simulacrum drops to 0 Hit Points, it shatters into ice and snow. You can create only one simulacrum at a time. Creating another ends the previous one.
