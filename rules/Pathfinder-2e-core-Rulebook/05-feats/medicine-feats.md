# Medicine Skill Feats

## Level 1 Feats

### Battle Medicine FEAT 1
**Prerequisites:** trained in Medicine  
Heal yourself or an ally in combat. Attempt a Medicine check with the same DC as for Treat Wounds and provide healing. Target is temporarily immune for 1 day.

## Level 2 Feats

### Continual Recovery FEAT 2
**Prerequisites:** expert in Medicine  
Treat Wounds on a patient more often; remove the 24-hour immunity between Treat Wounds attempts.

### Robust Recovery FEAT 2
**Prerequisites:** expert in Medicine  
Greater benefits from Treat Disease and Treat Poison; grants +2 circumstance bonus to saves.

### Ward Medic FEAT 2
**Prerequisites:** expert in Medicine  
Treat several patients at once; can treat up to 3 additional allies with a single check.

## Level 15 Feats

### Legendary Medic FEAT 15
**Prerequisites:** legendary in Medicine  
Remove disease or the blinded, deafened, doomed, or drained condition through medical treatment.
